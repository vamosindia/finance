1. 收益与风险基础 Return and Risk Fundamentals

收益率 (Return) 衡量每 1 美元投资在某段时间内的价值变化百分比，用于比较不同证券。
Return measures the percentage change in value of an investment per dollar initially invested over a period, allowing comparison across securities.

期望收益 / Expected Return

期望收益 (加权平均) / Expected Return (Weighted Average) E[R] = Σ pR × R
期望收益是若能多次重复投资、每次都从同一概率分布中抽取收益时所获得的平均回报。
Expected return is the average we would earn if we could repeat the investment many times, drawing returns from the same distribution each time.

风险度量：方差与标准差 / Variance and Standard Deviation

Var(R) = Σ pR × (R − E[R])2
SD(R) = √Var(R) （称为 volatility / 波动率）
方差衡量收益的离散程度。无风险资产的方差为零。
Variance measures how "spread out" the distribution of returns is. A risk-free asset has zero variance.
方差和标准差不区分上行和下行风险。替代度量包括：半方差 (semi-variance，仅考虑损失)、期望尾部损失 (expected tail loss，最差 x% 情景下的预期损失)。
Variance and SD do not differentiate upside vs downside risk. Alternatives: semi-variance (variance of losses only) and expected tail loss (expected loss in worst x% of outcomes).

2. 实际收益与估计误差 Realized Returns and Estimation Error

实际收益 (Realized Return)：在特定时段内实际发生的收益。
Realized return is the return that actually occurs over a particular time period.

由于现实中我们通常不知道精确的概率分布，常用做法是从历史数据外推：假设未来收益分布与过去类似，则历史平均收益可作为期望收益的估计。

估计的两个问题 / Two Issues with Estimation

  1. 我们无法观察过去投资者真实的预期，仅能观察实际发生的收益。
  2. 平均收益只是真实期望收益的一个估计，存在估计误差 (estimation error)。
标准误 / Standard Error (IID 假设下) SE = SD(独立观测值) / √(观测数)
95% 置信区间 / 95% Confidence Interval 平均收益 ± 1.96 × SE
个股波动性比组合更高、且存在时间往往较短，估计误差很大，因此个股的历史平均收益不是未来期望收益的可靠估计。
Individual stocks are more volatile than portfolios and may have short histories, so estimation error is large. Past average returns are not a reliable expectation for individual stocks.

3. 风险与收益的权衡 Risk-Return Trade-off

风险厌恶 / Risk Aversion

投资者在更高波动性的资产上要求更高的预期回报作为补偿——这就是风险厌恶。
Investors require higher expected returns to hold more volatile portfolios — this is risk aversion.

超额收益 / Excess Return (Risk Premium)

风险溢价 / Risk Premium 超额收益 = 投资平均收益 − 无风险投资平均收益 (国库券)
在组合层面，更高波动性对应更高平均收益（风险厌恶的实证证据）。但在个股层面，总风险（SD）与平均收益之间没有这种关系。
At the portfolio level, higher volatility corresponds to higher average returns. But at the individual stock level, total risk (SD) and average return show no such relationship.

4. 分散化与两类风险 Diversification and Two Types of Risk

公司特定风险 / Firm-Specific Risk
(idiosyncratic, unique, diversifiable)

由公司特定新闻引起的独立风险，可通过持有大量股票来分散消除。

Independent risks from firm-specific news; eliminated by holding a large diversified portfolio.

系统性风险 / Systematic Risk
(undiversifiable, market risk)

由市场范围新闻引起的共同风险，影响所有公司，无法通过分散化消除。

Common risks from market-wide news; affect all firms and cannot be diversified.

无套利论证：仅含公司特定风险的组合应赚取无风险利率。
→ 可分散风险的风险溢价为零，投资者不会因承担可分散风险而获补偿。
→ 风险溢价仅由系统性风险决定，与可分散风险无关。
No-arbitrage argument: a portfolio with only diversifiable risk should earn the risk-free rate. The risk premium for diversifiable risk is zero — investors are compensated only for systematic risk.
由于 SD 度量总风险（含可分散与系统两部分），不能用 SD 决定个股的风险溢价。但 SD 仍是良好分散化组合的合理风险度量。
Because SD measures total risk (both diversifiable and systematic), SD cannot be used to determine the risk premium of individual stocks. SD remains reasonable for well-diversified portfolios.

5. 系统性风险的度量：Beta Measuring Systematic Risk: Beta

有效组合 (Efficient Portfolio)：仅含系统性风险的组合，其风险无法通过进一步分散化减少而不降低期望收益。市场组合（全市场所有股票和证券，例如 S&P 500）是有效组合的自然候选。
An efficient portfolio contains only systematic risk and cannot be diversified further without lowering expected return. The market portfolio (all stocks and securities, e.g., S&P 500) is a natural candidate.
Beta 定义 / Definition of Beta βi = 证券 i (超额) 收益对市场 (超额) 收益每 1% 变动的期望百分比变化
Beta 值含义 / Interpretation
β = 1与市场组合同步波动 / Moves with the market
β > 1周期性行业，对系统风险更敏感 / Cyclical industries, more sensitive to systematic risk
0 < β < 1非周期性行业，对系统风险敏感性较低 / Non-cyclical, less sensitive
β < 0负风险溢价，期望收益低于无风险利率，提供"保险"功能 / Negative risk premium; provides insurance against systematic risk
Beta 是衡量证券风险的恰当指标，因为它与该证券对最优风险组合的边际风险贡献成正比。
Beta is the appropriate measure of risk because it is proportional to the risk the security contributes to the optimal risky portfolio.

6. 投资组合的收益与风险 Portfolio Return and Risk

组合期望收益 / Portfolio Expected Return E[RP] = Σ wi × E[Ri]

协方差与相关系数 / Covariance and Correlation

Cov(Ri, Rj) = E[(Ri − E[Ri])(Rj − E[Rj])]
Corr(Ri, Rj) = Cov(Ri, Rj) / (SDi × SDj)
相关系数控制了各股票的波动性，衡量它们之间关系的强度。相关系数不影响组合期望收益，但影响组合的波动率。两只完全负相关的股票可使组合风险为零。
Correlation controls for each stock's volatility, quantifying the strength of relationships. It does not affect portfolio return but affects portfolio volatility. Two perfectly negatively correlated stocks could yield zero risk.
两资产组合方差 / Two-Asset Portfolio Variance Var(RP) = w12Var1 + w22Var2 + 2w1w2Cov12
组合期望收益 = 加权平均期望收益；但组合波动率 < 加权平均波动率。这正是分散化效益的来源。20-30 只股票即可获得几乎全部分散化收益。
Portfolio expected return equals the weighted average return, but portfolio volatility is less than the weighted average volatility. This is the diversification benefit. 20-30 stocks achieve almost all benefits.

7. 有效前沿与切线组合 Efficient Frontier and Tangent Portfolio

有效前沿 / Efficient Frontier

在风险-收益图上，连接所有"在给定波动率下期望收益最高、或在给定期望收益下波动率最低"的组合的曲线（在最小方差组合之上的部分）即为有效前沿。增加股票数量可改善有效前沿。
The efficient frontier traces all portfolios with the highest expected return for each level of volatility (above the global minimum variance portfolio). Adding more stocks improves the frontier through greater diversification.

引入无风险资产 / Adding the Risk-Free Asset

E[RP] = (1 − x)rf + x · E[R风险组合]
SD(RP) = x · SD(R风险组合)
x > 1 时，意味着以无风险利率借款来加杠杆投资风险组合，称为保证金交易 (margin investing)。
When x > 1, the investor borrows at the risk-free rate to invest more in the risky portfolio — known as margin investing.

夏普比率 / Sharpe Ratio

夏普比率（报酬-波动率比） Sharpe Ratio = (E[RP] − rf) / SD(RP)
切线组合 (Tangent Portfolio)：从无风险资产出发的直线与有效前沿相切的点对应的组合，具有最高的夏普比率。任何投资者的最优配置都是切线组合与无风险资产的组合，且每个投资者的切线组合相同，差别只在于持有切线组合与无风险资产的权重。
The tangent portfolio is where the line from the risk-free asset is tangent to the efficient frontier — it has the highest Sharpe ratio. Every investor holds this tangent portfolio combined with the risk-free asset; only the weights differ based on risk preference.

8. 资本资产定价模型 CAPM The Capital Asset Pricing Model

CAPM 假设 / CAPM Assumptions

  1. 无税收、无交易成本 / No taxes, no transaction costs
  2. 可按 rf 借贷 / Borrowing/lending at rf
  3. 同质预期：所有投资者面对相同公开信息，对波动率、相关性、期望收益的估计相同 / Homogeneous expectations
  4. 投资者只持有有效组合 / Investors hold only efficient portfolios

核心结论 / Core Implication

在 CAPM 假设下，所有投资者使用相同输入，得到相同的切线组合。市场出清要求切线组合 = 市场组合。如果某资产不在市场组合中，则无人持有，价格下跌直到值得纳入。
Under CAPM, all investors use the same inputs and identify the same tangent portfolio. Market clearing requires tangent portfolio = market portfolio. If a stock weren't in it, no one would buy it, and its price would drop until inclusion is attractive.
连接无风险资产与市场组合的切线称为资本市场线 (Capital Market Line, CML)。
The line through the risk-free asset and the market portfolio is the Capital Market Line (CML).

CAPM 公式 / CAPM Formula

CAPM — 期望收益 / Expected Return E[Ri] = rf + βi × (E[RMkt] − rf)
rf 补偿"等待"（货币时间价值），风险溢价补偿"担忧"（投资回报的不确定性）。投资者不会因公司特定风险而获补偿。
rf compensates investors for "waiting" (time value of money); the risk premium compensates for "worrying" about investment returns. Investors are not compensated for firm-specific risk.

9. 证券市场线 SML 与 Alpha Security Market Line and Alpha

证券市场线 (SML)：所有"公允定价"的证券应该位于此线上的关于 (β, 期望收益) 的直线。市场组合的 β = 1，斜率即为市场风险溢价。
The Security Market Line plots expected return against beta. All "fairly priced" assets should lie on the SML. Market beta = 1, and the slope is the market risk premium.
位置 / Position含义 / Interpretation
SML 线上 / On SML公允定价 / Fairly priced (CAPM holds)
SML 线上方 / Above低估，期望收益超过 CAPM 要求 / Underpriced, positive alpha
SML 线下方 / Below高估，期望收益低于 CAPM 要求 / Overpriced, negative alpha

Alpha 与指数模型 / Alpha and the Index Model

指数模型 / Index Model Ri − rf = αi + βi(RM − rf) + ei
CAPM 预测在均衡时每只股票的 α = 0。若 α > 0，投资者买入推高价格直至 α 回到零；若 α < 0，投资者卖出压低价格直至 α 回到零。这一调整过程使所有 α 趋向 0。
CAPM predicts equilibrium α = 0 for every stock. Positive alpha attracts buyers, raising price and lowering expected return back to fair value; negative alpha causes selling. Rebalancing drives all alphas to zero.
组合 Beta / Portfolio Beta βP = Σ wi × βi

10. 必要回报率 Required Return

必要回报率 (Required Return)：补偿资产 i 给现有组合 P 带来的边际风险所需的期望收益。
Required return is the expected return necessary to compensate for the marginal risk that investment i contributes to portfolio P.
必要回报率 / Required Return ri = rf + βi,P(组合) × (E[RP] − rf)
如果资产 i 的期望收益 > 必要回报率，加入它将提高组合的夏普比率。最终的组合有效当且仅当每只证券的期望收益等于其必要回报率。
If E[Ri] > required return, adding more of i improves the Sharpe ratio. The final portfolio is efficient when expected return equals required return for every security.
这一条件等价于：可以用有效组合的 beta 来确定任何投资的恰当风险溢价。当 P = 市场组合时，这就是 CAPM。
This is equivalent to: the appropriate risk premium can be determined from beta with the efficient portfolio. When P = market portfolio, this is CAPM.

11. 股权资本成本估计 Estimating Equity Cost of Capital

资本成本 (Cost of Capital)：市场上具有相似风险与期限的投资可获得的最佳期望收益。
Cost of capital is the best expected return available in the market on investments with similar risk and term.
CAPM 应用于股权资本成本 / CAPM for Equity rE = rf + βE × (E[RMkt] − rf)

市场风险溢价的估计 / Estimating Market Risk Premium

方法一：历史平均超额收益。缺点：(1) 即使数年数据，标准误仍很大；(2) 回顾性，未必反映对未来的当前预期。
Method 1: Historical average excess return. Drawbacks: (1) large SE even with decades of data; (2) backward-looking, may not reflect current expectations.
方法二：基本面方法。用股息折现模型 (Gordon)：r = D1/P + g，反推市场期望回报，再减 rf。
Method 2: Fundamental approach. Using DDM (Gordon): r = D1/P + g, then subtract rf for the premium.

Beta 的估计 / Estimating Beta

  • 用 2-5 年的周或月度收益数据，以 S&P 500 为市场组合代理。
  • 绘制股票超额收益对市场超额收益的散点图，线性回归的斜率即为 β。
  • 该方法假设 β 在时间上相对稳定。
CAPM 给出的期望回报估计比股票历史平均回报更准确，因为它基于系统性风险而非样本噪音。
CAPM provides much more accurate estimates of expected returns than historical averages, because it is based on systematic risk rather than sample noise.

12. 债务资本成本 Cost of Debt

债务资本成本 (rD)：债权人要求的期望回报，即公司必须为其债务支付的资本成本。
Debt cost of capital is the expected return required by the firm's creditors.

方法一：YTM 与违约调整 / YTM and Default Adjustment

到期收益率 (YTM) 是若投资者持有至到期并收到承诺现金流时获得的 IRR。当违约概率 (PD) 很低时，YTM 是期望收益的良好估计；PD 较高时，YTM 高估期望收益（因承诺 ≠ 期望）。
YTM is the IRR an investor earns from holding the bond to maturity with all promised payments. When PD is low, YTM approximates expected return well. When PD is higher, YTM overstates expected return.
违约调整 / Default Adjustment rD = YTM − PD × L

L = 违约时的预期损失率 / Expected Loss given default

方法二：CAPM / CAPM Approach

用债券的 β 通过 CAPM 计算 rD。由于银行贷款和许多公司债交易不频繁，难以直接估计 β，可借用同评级债券指数的 β。
Estimate rD via CAPM using debt beta. Since bank loans and corporate bonds trade infrequently, use betas of bond indices in the same rating category as proxies.

13. 公司与项目资本成本 Company and Project Cost of Capital

公司资本成本是企业全部资产投资的机会成本，是平均风险项目的正确折现率。
The company cost of capital is the opportunity cost for an investment in all firm assets and is the correct discount rate for average-risk projects.

两种计算方法 / Two Methods

  1. 资产 Beta + CAPM：估计无杠杆 β，用 CAPM 计算资本成本。
  2. 加权平均：rU = (E/V)rE + (D/V)rD。第二种更常用，因为债务成本（借款利率）可观察，而债务 β 难直接估计。

项目资本成本的估计 / Project Cost of Capital

项目应独立评估，与融资决策分开。新项目不是公开交易证券，无历史数据。应识别同行业可比公司，使用其 β 作为估计。
Projects should be evaluated separately from financing decisions. New projects lack public-trading data, so identify comparable firms in the same line of business and use their betas.
如果可比公司是全股权，可直接用其股权 β 和股权资本成本作为项目估计。如果可比公司有杠杆，就需要先剥离杠杆得到资产 β（unlever），再应用到目标项目。
If the comparable is all-equity, use its equity beta and cost of equity directly. If levered, first unlever to obtain asset beta before applying it to the project.

14. 无杠杆资本成本与税后WACC Unlevered Cost of Capital and After-Tax WACC

资产资本成本 / 无杠杆资本成本 (rU)：投资者持有公司基础资产所要求的期望回报，等于公司在其项目上获得的平均回报率。
Asset cost of capital (unlevered cost of capital) is the expected return required to hold the firm's underlying assets — the average rate the company earns on projects.
无杠杆资本成本 = 资产组合期望收益 rU = (E/(E+D))·rE + (D/(E+D))·rD

净债务的使用 / Use of Net Debt

现金代表无风险资产，降低资产平均风险，相当于"负债务"。我们关心企业的核心业务风险，因此应该用企业价值 (Enterprise Value) = 股权 + 净债务，其中净债务 = 总债务 − 多余现金。
Cash is a risk-free asset that reduces firm risk and acts as "negative debt." We care about underlying business operations, so use enterprise value = equity + net debt, where net debt = total debt − excess cash.

税后 WACC / After-Tax WACC

税后 WACC / After-Tax WACC WACC = (E/V)·rE + (D/V)·rD·(1 − τc)
税后 WACC 低于无杠杆（机会）资本成本，因为它纳入了利息税盾的下行调整。利息支出可抵税意味着实际有效借款成本是 rD(1 − τc)。
After-tax WACC is lower than the unlevered cost of capital because it includes a tax adjustment. Interest deductibility means the effective after-tax cost of debt is rD(1 − τc).

WACC 总结 / WACC Summary

名称 / Term用途 / Use公式特征 / Formula
无杠杆资本成本 (税前 WACC) / Unlevered (Pretax) WACC 评估同等风险的全股权项目 / Evaluate all-equity project with same risk 使用税前 rD / Uses pretax rD
税后 WACC / After-tax WACC 评估同等风险且同等融资结构的项目 / Evaluate project with same risk AND same financing 使用税后 rD(1 − τc) / Uses after-tax rD
税后 WACC 公式仅在每期税盾恰好等于 D × rD时成立。如果存在不可全额抵扣的利息（如部分次级债），或 EBIT 不足以吸收税盾，则失效。同时假定股权融资无税盾效应。
After-tax WACC is valid only when the tax shield each period equals D × rD. It breaks down with non-deductible interest or insufficient EBIT, and assumes no tax shield from equity financing.

15. 口试可能问题精选 / Likely Oral Exam Questions

Q1. 基础 解释期望收益、方差、标准差的概念及其在投资分析中的作用。
Explain expected return, variance, and standard deviation, and their roles in investment analysis.
期望收益 E[R] = Σ pR × R，是若多次重复投资可获得的平均回报。方差 Var(R) = Σ pR × (R − E[R])2，衡量收益分布的"分散程度"。标准差 SD = √Var，单位与收益一致，常称波动率。这三者构成 Markowitz 均值-方差分析的基础，但无法区分上行与下行风险，因此在重视下行风险的语境下应使用半方差或期望尾部损失。
Expected return E[R] = Σp·R is the average return from repeating the investment. Variance measures the spread; SD is the standard deviation in original units. These form the basis of Markowitz mean-variance analysis but cannot distinguish upside from downside risk; semi-variance or expected tail loss are alternatives.
Q2. 基础 区分公司特定风险和系统性风险，并解释为什么投资者只会因系统性风险获得补偿。
Distinguish firm-specific and systematic risk, and explain why investors are only compensated for systematic risk.
公司特定风险（idiosyncratic）来源于公司特定事件（管理层变动、产品失败等），相互独立，可通过持有大量股票分散消除。
系统性风险（market）来源于市场范围因素（利率、经济周期、政策），同时影响所有公司，无法分散。
无套利论证：仅含可分散风险的组合应赚 rf。否则投资者构建该组合并卖空国库券即可套利。因此可分散风险的风险溢价为零；投资者只对承担系统性风险获得补偿。
Firm-specific risk comes from individual firm events and is independent across firms — it can be diversified away. Systematic risk comes from market-wide factors and affects all firms. The no-arbitrage argument shows a portfolio with only diversifiable risk should earn rf; therefore the premium for diversifiable risk is zero. Investors are compensated only for systematic risk.
Q3. 基础 什么是 Beta？为何它是衡量证券风险的恰当指标？
What is beta and why is it the appropriate measure of risk?
Beta βi 是证券 i 的(超额)收益对市场组合(超额)收益每 1% 变动的期望百分比变化。它仅度量系统性风险。市场组合 β = 1。Beta 是恰当的风险度量，因为它与该证券对最优风险组合的边际风险贡献成正比，正是投资者要求补偿的部分；而 SD 含可分散风险，并非市场定价的部分。
Beta is the expected % change in security i's (excess) return per 1% change in the market's (excess) return. It measures only systematic risk; market beta = 1. Beta is appropriate because it is proportional to the marginal risk a security contributes to the optimal risky portfolio — the part for which investors demand compensation. SD includes diversifiable risk, which is not priced.
Q4. 进阶 推导并解释 CAPM 公式。
Derive and explain the CAPM formula.
关键假设：无税无交易成本、可按 rf 借贷、同质预期、投资者只持有效组合。
逻辑：在同质预期下，所有投资者识别出相同的切线组合。市场出清要求切线组合 = 市场组合。每个证券的均衡定价应使其期望回报与对市场组合的 β 成正比：
E[Ri] = rf + βi(E[RMkt] − rf)
含义：rf 补偿"等待"，风险溢价补偿"担忧"系统性风险。
Assumptions: no taxes/costs, borrow/lend at rf, homogeneous expectations, investors hold only efficient portfolios. With homogeneous expectations, all investors identify the same tangent portfolio. Market clearing forces tangent = market portfolio. In equilibrium: E[Ri] = rf + βi(E[RM] − rf). rf compensates for waiting; the risk premium compensates for systematic risk.
Q5. 进阶 什么是有效组合和切线组合？为什么所有投资者都应该持有切线组合？
What are efficient and tangent portfolios? Why does every investor hold the tangent portfolio?
有效组合位于均值-方差有效前沿上：在给定波动率下回报最高，或在给定回报下波动率最低。
切线组合是从无风险资产出发的直线与有效前沿相切的点对应的组合，具有最高的夏普比率。
两基金分离定理：所有投资者只需持有切线组合 + 无风险资产的不同权重组合即可达到任意风险偏好下的最优配置。在 CAPM 同质预期下，切线组合对所有投资者都相同，必须等于市场组合（否则市场不出清）。
Efficient portfolios lie on the mean-variance efficient frontier: highest return for given volatility. The tangent portfolio is where the line from rf touches the frontier — it has the highest Sharpe ratio. By the Two-Fund Separation Theorem, every investor optimally combines the tangent portfolio with the risk-free asset. With CAPM's homogeneous expectations, all investors derive the same tangent portfolio, which must equal the market portfolio.
Q6. 基础 什么是夏普比率？它在投资决策中如何使用？
What is the Sharpe ratio and how is it used in decisions?
夏普比率 = (E[RP] − rf) / SD(RP)，衡量每单位总风险所获得的超额回报，是从风险-收益图的 rf 出发到组合 P 的直线的斜率。
用途：(1) 在两个组合间选择，斜率更高者更优；(2) 寻找最优风险组合时，目标是最大化夏普比率，得到切线组合；(3) 评估基金经理表现。
Sharpe ratio = (E[RP] − rf) / SD(RP) measures excess return per unit of total risk — the slope of the line from rf through P. Uses: (1) ranking portfolios; (2) identifying the tangent portfolio; (3) evaluating manager performance.
Q7. 基础 解释证券市场线 (SML) 与 alpha 的概念。
Explain the SML and alpha.
SML 是 (β, 期望收益) 平面上的直线，截距为 rf，斜率为市场风险溢价 (E[RM] − rf)。CAPM 预测所有公允定价资产位于 SML 上。
Alpha = 实际期望收益 − CAPM 预测的公允收益。α > 0 = 低估（在 SML 上方）；α < 0 = 高估（在 SML 下方）。在均衡中所有 α = 0：投资者买入正 α 股票推高价格直至 α 归零；卖出负 α 股票直至归零。
SML plots (β, E[R]) with intercept rf and slope = market risk premium. CAPM predicts fairly priced assets lie on SML. Alpha = actual E[R] − CAPM-predicted return. Positive alpha = above SML (underpriced); negative alpha = below SML (overpriced). In equilibrium all alphas → 0 through investor rebalancing.
Q8. 易混 区分有效前沿、CML 和 SML。
Distinguish efficient frontier, CML, and SML.
有效前沿 (Efficient Frontier)：风险资产组合的最优集合，绘制在 (SD, 期望收益) 空间，是一条曲线。
CML (Capital Market Line)：从 rf 出发与有效前沿相切于市场组合 M 的直线，绘制在 (SD, 期望收益) 空间。仅描述有效组合。
SML (Security Market Line)：绘制在 (β, 期望收益) 空间，描述所有证券（包括非有效组合）的均衡定价。
关键差别：CML 用 SD（适用于有效组合），SML 用 β（适用于所有资产）。
Efficient frontier: optimal portfolios of risky assets in (SD, E[R]) space — a curve. CML: line from rf tangent to frontier at M, in (SD, E[R]) space, describes only efficient portfolios. SML: in (β, E[R]) space, describes equilibrium pricing of all securities (efficient or not). Key difference: CML uses SD (efficient portfolios only), SML uses β (all assets).
Q9. 进阶 CAPM 的主要假设是什么？放松它们会有什么后果？
What are CAPM's main assumptions, and what happens if they are relaxed?
主要假设：无税无交易成本；可按 rf 借贷；同质预期；投资者只持有效组合。
放松效应：
(1) 不能以 rf 借款 → Black 的零β CAPM 替代；
(2) 异质预期 → 无单一最优组合，但可证明聚合关系仍成立；
(3) 多期消费决策 → ICAPM (Merton) 引入额外状态变量；
(4) 实证异象（规模、价值、动量）→ Fama-French 三因子、Carhart 四因子等多因子模型。
Main assumptions: no taxes/costs; borrow/lend at rf; homogeneous expectations; investors hold only efficient portfolios. Relaxations: (1) no risk-free borrowing → Black's zero-beta CAPM; (2) heterogeneous expectations → aggregate relationship may still hold; (3) multiperiod consumption → Merton's ICAPM; (4) empirical anomalies (size, value, momentum) → Fama-French and Carhart multifactor models.
Q10. 基础 如何用 CAPM 估计股权资本成本？
How do you estimate cost of equity using CAPM?
步骤：
1. rf：用与项目期限匹配的国库券或长期国债收益率。
2. 市场风险溢价 (E[RM] − rf)：可用历史平均超额收益（缺点：SE 大、回顾性）或基本面方法（如 r = D1/P + g − rf）。
3. βE：用 2-5 年的周/月度数据，将股票超额收益对市场超额收益做线性回归，斜率即 β。
4. 代入 rE = rf + βE(E[RM] − rf)。
Steps: (1) rf from Treasury yields matching the horizon; (2) market risk premium from historical excess returns (large SE, backward-looking) or fundamentals (r = D1/P + g − rf); (3) β from 2-5 years of weekly/monthly returns, regressing stock excess returns on market excess returns; (4) apply rE = rf + βE(E[RM] − rf).
Q11. 进阶 为什么 YTM 不总等于债务资本成本？如何调整？
Why doesn't YTM always equal cost of debt? How is it adjusted?
YTM 是承诺现金流下的 IRR，假设无违约。当违约概率 (PD) 不可忽略时，期望现金流 < 承诺现金流，因此YTM 高估期望回报。
调整：rD ≈ YTM − PD × L，其中 L 为违约时的预期损失率。
PD 数据可从信用评级机构（Moody's、S&P）获取的违约率表中查找。
替代：用 CAPM + 同评级债券指数的 β 来估计 rD。
YTM is the IRR on promised cash flows, assuming no default. When PD is non-trivial, expected cash flows fall short of promised, so YTM overstates expected return. Adjustment: rD ≈ YTM − PD × L, where L is loss given default. PD comes from rating agency tables. Alternative: CAPM with bond-index betas of the same rating.
Q12. 易混 区分股权资本成本、债务资本成本、资产资本成本、WACC。
Distinguish cost of equity, cost of debt, cost of assets, and WACC.
rE 股权资本成本：股东要求的回报，反映杠杆放大的股权风险。
rD 债务资本成本：债权人要求的回报。
rU 资产 / 无杠杆资本成本：基础业务资产的回报，等于 D 和 E 的市值加权平均（不考虑税）。也称税前 WACC。
税后 WACC：rU 因利息税盾下调，= (E/V)rE + (D/V)rD(1−τc)。
关系：在完美市场（无税）rU = WACC；有税情况下 WACC < rU。
rE: required return to shareholders, reflects leverage. rD: required return to creditors. rU (asset/unlevered cost): return on underlying business — market-value-weighted average of E and D (= pretax WACC). After-tax WACC: rU reduced by tax shield = (E/V)rE + (D/V)rD(1−τc). In perfect markets rU = WACC; with taxes WACC < rU.
Q13. 进阶 为什么评估项目时要使用项目特定的资本成本而非公司 WACC？
Why use a project-specific cost of capital rather than company WACC?
公司 WACC 仅适用于具有公司平均风险的项目。如果项目风险高于平均，用 WACC 折现会高估 NPV，导致接受劣质项目；如果项目风险低于平均，会低估 NPV，导致放弃好项目。
正确做法：找到与项目相同风险特征的可比公司（pure-play），使用它们的资产 β（如有杠杆需先 unlever）来估计项目资本成本。
Company WACC is appropriate only for projects with average company risk. Using it for higher-risk projects overstates NPV and accepts bad projects; for lower-risk projects, understates NPV and rejects good ones. Use pure-play comparables and their (unlevered) asset betas to estimate project-specific cost of capital.
Q14. 易混 为什么使用净债务而非总债务来计算资产 β 和 WACC？
Why use net debt instead of total debt for asset beta and WACC?
现金及短期投资是无风险资产，降低公司整体风险，相当于"负债务"。我们关心的是核心业务运营风险，不是公司携带多少现金。
使用企业价值 = 股权 + 净债务（净债务 = 总债务 − 多余现金）能更准确地反映业务的系统风险。否则一家持有大量现金但有少量长期债务的公司，会被错误地视为高杠杆。
Cash and short-term investments are risk-free, reducing overall risk and acting as "negative debt." We care about underlying business risk, not cash holdings. Use enterprise value = equity + net debt (net debt = total debt − excess cash) to accurately reflect operating risk. Otherwise a cash-rich firm with modest long-term debt would appear over-levered.
Q15. 进阶 为什么 CAPM 估计期望收益比历史平均收益更准确？
Why does CAPM estimate expected returns more accurately than historical averages?
历史平均的两大问题：(1) 估计误差大，标准误 = SD/√n，即使数十年数据 SE 仍可能 ±2-3%；(2) 样本噪音，个股历史回报受公司特定事件主导。
CAPM 的优势：将估计误差集中在市场风险溢价这一个量上（用整个市场组合估计 SE 较小），加上稳定的 β 估计，得到的期望收益更精确。
简言之：估计市场期望回报比估计单个股票期望回报容易得多。
Historical averages suffer from (1) large estimation error (SE = SD/√n, still ±2-3% with decades of data); (2) sample noise dominated by firm-specific events. CAPM concentrates estimation error in one quantity — the market risk premium — which is more precisely estimated using the entire market portfolio. Combined with stable beta estimates, CAPM gives more accurate expected returns.
Q16. 进阶 解释必要回报率与期望回报的关系，以及它们如何决定有效组合。
Explain the relationship between required return and expected return, and how they determine an efficient portfolio.
必要回报率：补偿资产 i 给现有组合 P 带来的边际系统风险所需的回报，rireq = rf + βi,P(E[RP] − rf)。
规则：若 E[Ri] > 必要回报，加入更多 i 可提高组合夏普比率（因带来的边际回报超过对其风险贡献的补偿）；若 E[Ri] < 必要回报，应减持。
有效条件：组合有效当且仅当每只证券的期望收益等于其必要回报。当 P = 市场组合时，这就是 CAPM。
Required return compensates for the marginal systematic risk i adds to portfolio P: rireq = rf + βi,P(E[RP] − rf). If E[Ri] > required, increase i to raise Sharpe ratio. The portfolio is efficient iff E[R] = required return for every security. When P = market, this is CAPM.
Q17. 易混 何时使用税前 WACC，何时使用税后 WACC？
When to use pretax vs after-tax WACC?
税前 WACC (= rU)：评估全股权融资项目（不享有利息税盾）。例如：MM 完美市场分析、APV 法折现 FCF。
税后 WACC：评估与公司具有相同风险且相同融资结构的项目（享有利息税盾）。例如：FCF 折现法的标准 WACC 估值。
注意：使用税后 WACC 时不要再单独计算税盾的 PV，否则双重计算。
Pretax WACC (= rU): evaluate all-equity-financed projects (no tax shield). Used in MM analysis and APV. After-tax WACC: evaluate projects with same risk AND same financing as the firm (tax shield embedded). Used in standard DCF/FCF valuation. Caution: with after-tax WACC, do not separately add PV of tax shield — double counting.
Q18. 进阶 杠杆如何影响股权 β 和股权资本成本？
How does leverage affect equity beta and cost of equity?
资产 β（无杠杆 β）= (E/V)βE + (D/V)βD，反映纯业务风险。
重排得：βE = βU + (D/E)(βU − βD)。
含义：杠杆放大股权 β。即使债务无风险 (βD = 0)，βE = βU(1 + D/E) 也线性增加。代入 CAPM 即得 MM II：rE = rU + (D/E)(rU − rD)。
含税情形下需修正：βU = (E/V)βE + (D/V)βD(1 − τc) 等。
Asset beta = (E/V)βE + (D/V)βD reflects pure business risk. Rearranged: βE = βU + (D/E)(βU − βD). Even with risk-free debt, βE grows linearly with D/E. Combining with CAPM gives MM II: rE = rU + (D/E)(rU − rD). With taxes the unlever formula is adjusted accordingly.
Q19. 进阶 为什么 CAPM 中切线组合必须等于市场组合？
Why must the tangent portfolio equal the market portfolio under CAPM?
在 CAPM 同质预期下，所有投资者使用相同输入（期望收益、方差、协方差），识别出相同的切线组合。每个投资者只持有切线组合 + 无风险资产，即所有投资者持有的风险资产都是相同比例的切线组合。
市场出清要求总需求 = 总供给（所有发行在外的证券）。因此切线组合的成分权重必须等于市场组合中各资产的市值权重。
若某资产权重在切线组合中为零，则无人买入，价格下跌直至期望回报上升、足以纳入切线组合，市场重新均衡。
Under homogeneous expectations, all investors derive the same tangent portfolio. Each holds tangent + rf; thus everyone's risky holdings are proportional to the tangent portfolio. Market clearing requires aggregate demand = supply (all outstanding securities). So tangent weights must equal market-cap weights. If an asset had zero weight, no one would hold it; its price would drop until its expected return rises enough for inclusion.
Q20. 易混 为什么 CAPM 仍有用，尽管实证检验中常被拒绝？
Why is CAPM still useful despite frequent empirical rejection?
实证问题：(1) 真实"市场组合"不可观察（Roll 批判）；(2) 异常回报与 size、value、momentum 相关，CAPM 单因子不足；(3) β 的稳定性受时间影响。
然而 CAPM 仍是基准：
(1) 提供清晰的风险-回报关系框架，便于直觉理解；
(2) 实务中仍是最常用的资本成本估计方法（McKinsey、Goldman 等机构调查）；
(3) 多因子模型（Fama-French 三因子等）是 CAPM 的扩展而非替代；
(4) 即使不完全准确，也比"猜"或纯历史平均更系统。
简言之：CAPM 不是真理，而是有用的近似。
Empirical issues: (1) the true "market portfolio" is unobservable (Roll's critique); (2) anomalies (size, value, momentum) suggest one factor is insufficient; (3) beta is unstable. Yet CAPM remains the benchmark: (1) clear risk-return framework; (2) most widely used in practice; (3) multifactor models (Fama-French) extend rather than replace CAPM; (4) even imperfect, it is more systematic than guessing or pure historical averaging. CAPM is not truth but a useful approximation.

🎯 口试应答策略 / Oral Exam Strategy

  1. 由概念到公式：先解释什么是"风险厌恶""分散化""系统性风险"，再引入 Beta、CAPM 等定量工具。
  2. 始终强调"系统性 vs 可分散"：这是整个理论的核心二分。每次提到风险溢价时声明"仅补偿系统性风险"。
  3. 区分四种资本成本：rE、rD、rU（=税前 WACC）、税后 WACC。教授常追问公式与适用场景。
  4. CAPM 假设：背熟四条核心假设（无税无成本、可按 rf 借贷、同质预期、只持有效组合），并能解释每条放松后的影响。
  5. 实证局限要诚实承认：CAPM 在实证中常被拒绝（Fama-French 三因子等扩展），但仍是实务标准框架。
  6. 结合资本结构知识：若被问到 rE，主动引出 MM II（杠杆与股权风险关系）；若被问到 WACC，主动引出税盾。
