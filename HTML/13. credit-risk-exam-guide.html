1. 信用风险基础概念 Credit Risk Fundamentals
信用风险定义 | Credit Risk Definition:
信用风险是由于交易对手未能履约而导致损失的风险,通常是银行业风险的最大组成部分。
Credit risk is the risk of loss arising from the non-performance of counterparties, typically the largest component of banking risk.

信用风险子类型 | Credit Risk Subtypes

1. 信用违约风险 | Credit Default Risk

与特定金融或投资服务活动(如贷款、借款、融资租赁)相关的信用风险。

Credit risk associated with financial/investment services activities (e.g., lending, borrowing, leasing).

2. 交易对手信用风险 | Counterparty Credit Risk

在交易最终结算前,交易对手未能履行合约义务所产生的潜在损失(通常指衍生品交易)。

Potential loss from counterparty failure to meet obligations before final settlement (typically derivatives).

3. 发行人信用风险 | Issuer Credit Risk

债券和股票发行人的信用风险,包括因发行人信用质量恶化而导致证券价值下降的风险。

Risk that security value declines due to deterioration in issuer's credit quality.

4. 国家风险 | Country Risk

与特定国家或地区相关的信用风险。

Credit risk associated with specific countries or regions.

核心信用风险指标 | Key Credit Risk Metrics

违约概率 | Probability of Default (PD)
PD = 在给定时间段内(如一年)发生违约事件的概率
Probability that a default event will occur within a given time period (e.g., one year)
违约损失率 | Loss Given Default (LGD)
LGD = 1 - 回收率 (Recovery Rate)
Percentage of loss in case of default
违约风险暴露 | Exposure at Default (EAD)
EAD = 违约时未偿还债权的总额
Total amount of claims outstanding at the time of default
预期信用损失 | Expected Credit Loss (ECL)
ECL = PD × LGD × EAD
Expected (Credit) Loss = Probability of Default × Loss Given Default × Exposure at Default
💡 口试要点: 能够清晰解释这四个核心指标的含义及其在信用风险管理中的作用。准备实际案例说明如何计算ECL。
Oral Exam Tip: Be able to clearly explain these four core metrics and their role in credit risk management. Prepare practical examples of ECL calculation.
2. 违约概率估计方法 Methods for Estimating Default Probability

方法一: 历史数据法 | Historical Data Method

数据来源 | Data Sources:
• 评级机构数据 (如Moody's, S&P, Fitch)
• 银行内部评级数据
• Rating agency data (e.g., Moody's, S&P, Fitch)
• Banks' internal rating data

外部信用评级 | External Credit Ratings

AAA AA A BBB BB B CCC

评级机构提供描述公司债券信用质量的评级。评级变更不频繁,仅在公司信用质量发生长期变化时才修订。

Rating agencies provide ratings describing creditworthiness of corporate bonds. Ratings are revised infrequently, only when long-term creditworthiness changes.

累积平均违约率表 | Cumulative Average Default Rates

示例: 初始评级为Baa的公司:

  • 第1年末违约概率: 0.174%
  • 第2年末违约概率: 0.504%
  • 第3年末违约概率: 更高...

Example: Company with initial Baa rating:

  • End of Year 1: 0.174% default probability
  • End of Year 2: 0.504% default probability
⚠️ 重要规律 | Important Pattern:
• 投资级债券: PD随时间递增(更可能在未来某年出现财务问题)
• 低评级债券: PD随时间递减(如果能度过最初1-2年危险期则情况改善)

• Investment-grade bonds: PD increases with time (greater chance of problems in some future year)
• Poor credit rating bonds: PD decreases with time (if survives critical first 1-2 years, conditions improve)
风险率 (Hazard Rate) 或违约强度
λ(t) = 无条件违约概率(第t年) / 存活到第t年的概率
Hazard Rate = Unconditional default probability during year t / Probability of surviving until year t
回收率 | Recovery Rate
回收率 = 违约后30天的债券交易价格 / 面值
Recovery Rate = 1 - LGD
Recovery Rate = Bond price 30 days after default / Face value
Recovery Rate = 1 - LGD
负相关关系: 回收率与违约率呈负相关。违约率低的年份经济状况通常良好,回收率较高;反之亦然。
Negative correlation: Recovery rate is negatively related to default rates. In years with low defaults, economic conditions are good and recovery rates are higher.

方法二: 信用利差法 | Credit Spreads Method

信用违约互换 (CDS) | Credit Default Swap

定义: CDS是允许市场参与者交易信用风险的衍生品,为买方提供针对特定公司违约风险的保险。

Definition: CDS is a derivative allowing participants to trade credit risks, providing insurance against default of a specific company.

CDS机制 | CDS Mechanism:
• 买方: 定期支付保费(通常按季度),获得违约保护
• 卖方: 收取保费,在信用事件发生时赔付
• 参考实体: 被保险的公司
• 信用事件: 未能支付、债务重组或破产

• Buyer: Makes periodic premium payments, gets default protection
• Seller: Receives premiums, pays in case of credit event
• Reference entity: The insured company
• Credit event: Failure to pay, debt restructuring, or bankruptcy
CDS利差 | CDS Spread
CDS利差 = 每年支付的总额 / 名义本金 (百分比)
CDS Spread = Total annual payment as % of notional principal
从信用利差预测PD | Predicting PD from Credit Spreads
平均风险率 (Average Hazard Rate):
λ̄(0,t) ≈ s(t) / (1 - R)
其中 s(t) = 期限为t的信用利差, R = 回收率

Where s(t) = credit spread for maturity t, R = recovery rate
1年CDS的违约概率 | 1-Year CDS Default Probability
PD(1年) = CDS利差(1年) / LGD
PD(1-year) = CDS spread(1-year) / LGD
💡 CDS与债券收益率利差的关系:
n年期CDS利差应约等于n年期公司债券票面收益率与n年期无风险债券票面收益率之差。否则存在套利机会。
The n-year CDS spread should approximately equal the excess of the par yield on an n-year corporate bond over the par yield on an n-year risk-free bond. Otherwise, arbitrage opportunities exist.
⚠️ 信息不对称: CDS与其他衍生品不同,因为某些市场参与者可能比其他人更了解违约概率,存在信息不对称问题。
Asymmetric Information: Unlike other derivatives, some market participants may have better information to estimate default probability than others.

方法三: 结构化(股权)模型 | Structured (Equity) Models

核心优势: 股票价格提供更及时的信息来估计PD,因为信用评级变更不频繁,仅在公司信用状况发生长期变化时才修订。
Key Advantage: Equity prices provide more up-to-date information for estimating PD because credit ratings are revised infrequently.

默顿模型 (Merton Model) 🌟

期权定价理论 Black-Scholes 看涨期权
核心思想: 公司股东持有公司资产的看涨期权,行权价等于债务面值D。
Core Idea: Company shareholders have a long call option on company assets with strike price equal to debt D.

模型假设 | Model Assumptions:

  • 公司资产可在无摩擦市场交易 | Assets tradeable in frictionless markets
  • 无风险利率恒定 | Risk-free rate constant over time
  • 公司资产价值遵循几何布朗运动(对数正态分布) | Asset value follows GBM (lognormal distribution)
  • 公司不能回购或发行新的优先债务 | Firm cannot repurchase or issue new senior debt
股权到期价值 | Equity Value at Maturity
ET = max(VT - D, 0)
• 如果 VT < D: 发生违约,股权价值为0
• 如果 VT ≥ D: 股权价值 = VT - D

• If VT < D: Default occurs, equity value = 0
• If VT ≥ D: Equity value = VT - D
Black-Scholes期权定价公式 | Black-Scholes Formula
E0 = V0 × N(d1) - D × e-rT × N(d2)

d1 = [ln(V0/D) + (r + σV2/2)T] / (σV√T)

d2 = d1 - σV√T

Where V0 = firm value, D = debt, r = risk-free rate, σV = asset volatility
违约概率 | Probability of Default
PD = N(-d2) = 1 - N(d2)

PD = N[{ln(DT/VT) - (r - σV2/2)T} / (σV√T)]

This is the probability that the call option is not exercised (default occurs)
🔍 如何求解V0和σV?
由于V0和σV不可观测,但E0(股价)可观测:
1. 通过历史股价计算σE
2. 使用伊藤引理(Ito's Lemma)建立关系:
σE × E0 = σV × V0 × N(d1)
3. 联立Black-Scholes公式和伊藤引理方程求解

Since V0 and σV are unobservable but E0 (stock price) is observable:
Use Ito's Lemma to establish relationship and solve simultaneously with Black-Scholes formula
⚠️ 风险中性 vs 真实世界概率:
默顿模型计算的PD理论上是风险中性概率,因为它来自期权定价模型。风险中性世界的PD高于真实世界,因为预期增长率是无风险利率rf,低于真实世界中公司资产的增长率。

The PD calculated is theoretically a risk-neutral probability since it comes from an option pricing model. Risk-neutral PD is higher than real-world PD because expected growth rate is rf, which is lower than real-world asset growth rate.
违约概率的影响因素:
• PD 增加: 债务DT↑, 资产波动率σV↑
• PD 减少: 初始资产价值V0↑

Factors affecting PD:
• PD increases with: debt DT↑, asset volatility σV↑
• PD decreases with: initial asset value V0↑

⚠️ 模型缺陷 | Disadvantages

  • 假设正态分布,未考虑极端或罕见事件
  • 违约只能在到期日发生,但现实中可以提前违约
  • 违约等同于公司清算,未考虑重组选项
  • Assumes normal distribution, doesn't account for extreme events
  • Default occurs only at maturity, but can occur earlier in reality
  • Default corresponds to liquidation, doesn't consider reorganization
    •

    ✓ 模型优势 | Advantages

    • 允许使用期权定价公式评估违约概率和回收率
    • 最适合股票公开交易的公司
    • 提供理论框架理解信用风险
  • Allows use of option pricing formulas to evaluate PD and recovery rate
  • Best suited for publicly traded companies
  • Provides theoretical framework for understanding credit risk
    •

    KMV模型 (Moody's) 🌟

    违约距离 预期违约频率 真实世界概率
    核心创新: KMV建立在默顿模型基础上,使用"违约距离"(Distance to Default)概念,并将风险中性概率转换为真实世界违约概率(预期违约频率EDF)。
    Core Innovation: KMV builds on Merton model using "Distance to Default" concept and transforms risk-neutral probability into real-world default probability (Expected Default Frequency, EDF).
    违约距离 (DD) | Distance to Default
    DD = d2

    DD = [ln(V0/D) + (r - σV2/2)T] / (σV√T)

    简化版: DD = (Vt - D) / (σV × Vt)

    DD is the number of standard deviations the asset price must change for default to be triggered T years in the future.
    Simplified: DD = (Vt - D) / (σV × Vt)
    预期违约频率 (EDF) | Expected Default Frequency
    EDF = Φ(-DD)

    风险中性世界: EDF = N(-d2) = 1 - N(d2)

    EDF is a forward-looking measure of actual probability of default
    Risk-neutral world: EDF = N(-d2) = 1 - N(d2)
    🔬 EDF校准方法:
    基于大量公司样本的历史信息,对于每个时间范围,可以估计给定违约距离(如DD = 4.0)的公司在一年后实际违约的比例。这将风险中性概率映射为真实世界概率。

    Based on historical information on large sample of firms, for each time horizon, estimate the proportion of firms with a given default distance (e.g., DD = 4.0) that actually defaulted after one year. This maps risk-neutral to real-world probability.

    ✓ KMV优势 | KMV Advantages

    • EDF变化通常比评级机构降级提前至少一年
    • 允许在一年内违约
    • 提供比定期更新的信用评级更及时的信息
  • Changes in EDF tend to anticipate downgrades by at least one year
  • Allows default within one year
  • Provides more up-to-date information than periodically updated ratings
    •

    Black-Cox模型 (首次通过法) | Black-Cox Model (First-Passage Approach)

    连续监控 障碍期权
    核心改进: 在默顿模型中,公司价值可以降至几乎为零而不触发违约。Black-Cox模型允许在公司资产价值首次跌破债务面值时就发生违约,而不仅仅在到期日。

    Core Improvement: In Merton model, firm value can drop to almost nothing without default. Black-Cox argues that default occurs at the first time firm's asset value drops below debt face value, not just at maturity.

    安全条款 | Safety Covenant:

    债券持有人有权行使"安全条款",允许他们在公司价值任何时候跌破指定阈值K(t)时清算公司。

    Bondholders have the right to exercise "safety covenant" that allows them to liquidate firm if value drops below threshold K(t) at any time.

    违约逻辑:
    • 如果公司价值从未跌破障碍D (D ≥ K): 债券投资者获得面值K,股权持有人获得VT - K
    • 如果公司价值在债券期限内跌破障碍 (VT ≤ D): 公司违约,债券投资者接管资产D,股权投资者一无所获

    Default Logic:
    • If firm value never falls below barrier D: bondholders receive K, equity holders receive VT - K
    • If firm value falls below barrier: firm defaults, bondholders take over assets, equity holders receive nothing
    💡 债券保护: 如果D ≥ K,债券投资者完全受保护,至少获得面值K。但如果D < K,债券持有人仍面临违约风险。
    Bond Protection: If D ≥ K, bond investors are fully protected, receiving at least face value K. If D < K, bondholders are exposed to default risk.

    Leland模型 (内生违约点) | Leland Model (Endogenous Default Point)

    管理者决策 动态权衡理论
    核心特征: 在此模型中,公司所有者/管理者决定何时破产。模型纳入动态权衡理论:同时考虑税收优惠和财务困境成本。

    Core Feature: In this model, firm owners/managers decide when to go into bankruptcy. Incorporates dynamic trade-off theory: tax benefits and cost of financial distress are considered.

    最优阈值选择: 所有者选择最优阈值VB,使股权价值最高。

    Optimal Threshold Selection: Owners choose optimal threshold VB to maximize equity value.

    💡 口试策略 | Oral Exam Strategy:
    准备比较这三种方法的优缺点。能够解释为什么在不同情况下选择不同方法。特别关注默顿模型和KMV模型的区别,以及如何从风险中性概率转换为真实世界概率。

    Prepare to compare advantages and disadvantages of these three methods. Be able to explain why different methods are chosen in different situations. Focus especially on differences between Merton and KMV models, and how to convert from risk-neutral to real-world probabilities.
    3. 信用风险模型 Credit Risk Models
    违约相关性 | Default Correlation:
    违约相关性描述两家公司大约同时违约的倾向(例如,同一行业或同一地区的公司往往受到外部事件的类似影响)。违约相关性意味着信用风险不能完全通过多元化消除,这是风险中性违约概率大于真实世界违约概率的主要原因。

    Default correlation describes the tendency for two companies to default at about the same time (e.g., companies in same industry/region tend to be affected similarly by external events). Default correlation means credit risk cannot be completely diversified away, and is the major reason why risk-neutral default probabilities are greater than real-world default probabilities.

    三种主要量化方法 | Three Main Quantitative Approaches

    方法 | Approach 核心思想 | Core Idea 代表模型 | Representative Models
    结构化方法
    Structural Approach    		 对公司资产动态、资本结构、债务和股东做出明确假设。公司在资产不足时违约。公司负债可表征为公司资产的期权。
    Explicit assumptions about firm's asset dynamics, capital structure. Firm defaults if assets are insufficient. Corporate liability characterized as option on firm's assets.    		 Merton, Vasicek (KMV), Black-Cox
    简约形式方法
    Reduced Form Approach    		 对公司为何违约保持沉默。违约动态通过违约率或强度外生给出。信用敏感证券的价格可以按无风险方式计算,使用由强度调整的利率。
    Silent about why firm defaults. Dynamics of default exogenously given through default rate/intensity. Prices calculated as if default-free using intensity-adjusted interest rate.    		 Survival Models, Counting Process Models
    不完全信息方法
    Incomplete Information Approach    		 结合两种方法的最佳特征。
    Combines best features of both approaches.    		 混合模型 | Hybrid Models

    结构化模型详解 | Structural Models in Detail

    基本理念: 公司负债是公司资产的或有索取权。公司市值是驱动信用风险的根本不确定性来源。这些模型用资产价值低于阈值来解释公司违约。

    Basic Philosophy: Corporate liabilities are contingent claims on firm's assets. Market value of firm is fundamental source of uncertainty driving credit risk. These models explain defaults with asset value falling below a threshold.

    ✓ 结构化模型优势 | Advantages

    • 允许使用期权定价公式(如Black-Scholes)评估违约概率和回收率
    • 提供违约的经济解释
    • 最适合股票公开交易的公司
  • Allow use of option pricing formulas to evaluate PD and recovery rate
  • Provide economic explanation of default
  • Best suited for publicly traded companies
    •
    违约相关性引入: A公司和B公司之间的违约相关性通过假设A公司资产遵循的随机过程与B公司资产遵循的随机过程相关来引入。
    Default correlation between company A and B is introduced by assuming the stochastic process followed by A's assets is correlated with the stochastic process followed by B's assets.

    简约形式模型 | Reduced Form Models

    核心假设: 假设违约随机发生,无预警,具有外生违约率或强度。不追问公司为何违约(不对违约原因做假设),强度模型从市场价格校准。

    Core Assumption: Assume default occurs randomly, without warning at exogenous default rate/intensity. Instead of asking why firm defaults (no assumption on causes), intensity model is calibrated from market prices.

    生存模型 | Survival Models

    生存函数 | Survivor Function S(t)
    S(t) = P(τ > t)
    Probability that default time τ is greater than specific time t
    Typically decreasing function, starting from 1 at t=0, approaching 0 as t increases
    累积概率分布函数 | Cumulative Distribution Function F(t)
    F(t) = 1 - S(t) = P(τ ≤ t)
    Probability that default occurs on or before time t
    Starts at 0, increases to 1 as time progresses
    概率密度函数 | Probability Density Function f(t)
    f(t) = dF(t)/dt = -dS(t)/dt
    Likelihood of default occurring at specific time t
    Derivative of CDF
    风险率 | Hazard Rate λ(t)
    λ(t) = f(t) / S(t)
    Instantaneous rate of default at time t, given survival up to that point
    Also known as intensity or default intensity

    计数过程 | Counting Process N(t)

    定义: 计数过程是表示截至给定时间t累积发生的事件(违约)数量的随机过程。从t=0时的0开始,每次违约事件发生时增加1。

    Definition: Stochastic process representing cumulative number of events (defaults) that have occurred up to time t. Starts at 0 at t=0, increases by 1 each time default occurs.

    性质 | Properties:

    • 过程随时间非递减 | Non-decreasing over time
    • 右连续 | Right-continuous
    • N(t) = 0 当 t < τ, N(t) = 1 当 t > τ

    与风险率的关系 | Relationship with Hazard Rate:

    在小时间间隔dt内,计数过程的预期变化约为 λ(t) × dt

    Expected change in counting process over small interval dt is approximately λ(t) × dt

    泊松过程: N(t)是具有齐次λ参数的泊松过程。

    N(t) is a Poisson process with homogenous λ parameter.

    ✓ 简约形式模型优势 | Advantages

    • 使用观察数据意味着可以采用历史估计程序来确定信用风险度量
    • 信用风险随商业周期变化
    • 无需指定公司资产负债表
  • Use of observed data means historical estimation procedures can be employed
  • Credit risk varies with business cycle
  • No need to specify firm's balance sheet
    •

    ⚠️ 简约形式模型缺陷 | Disadvantages

    • 能够实现的违约相关性范围有限
    • 过去的市场条件可能不反映未来
    • 未提供违约发生的经济解释
    • 将违约视为随机事件,而实际并非如此
  • Range of default correlations that can be achieved is limited
  • Past market conditions may not reflect future
  • Do not provide economic explanation of why default occurs
  • Treat default as random event, which is not the case in reality
    •
    4. 信用风险价值 (Credit VaR) Credit Value at Risk
    CVaR定义: 信用风险VaR是在一定置信区间下,某一时期内不会超过的信用风险损失。CVaR的时间范围通常比市场风险VaR更长(1年 vs 1天)。

    CVaR Definition: Credit risk VaR is the credit risk loss over a certain period of time that will not be exceeded with a certain confidence interval. Time horizon for CVaR is often longer than Market risk VaR (1 year vs 1 day).
    🎯 两个主要用途:
    1. 监管资本 (Regulatory Capital) - 监管机构要求
    2. 经济资本 (Economic Capital) - 银行自身对所承担风险的资本估计

    Two Main Uses:
    1. Regulatory Capital - Required by regulators
    2. Economic Capital - Bank's own estimate of capital for risk taking
    ⚠️ 信用相关性的关键作用:
    CVaR的一个重要方面是信用相关性。随着信用相关性增加(主要在压力经济条件下),公司更可能违约,因为它们受到经济因素的类似影响。

    Important Aspect of CVaR: Credit correlation. As credit correlation increases (mostly in stressed economic conditions), companies become more likely to default as they are similarly affected by economic factors.

    CreditMetrics和评级转移矩阵 🌟

    核心方法: 由摩根大通提出的CreditMetrics方法基于转移矩阵。这些矩阵显示公司在一定时期内从一个评级类别迁移到另一个评级类别的概率,基于评级机构产生的历史数据。

    Core Method: CreditMetrics approach proposed by JPMorgan is based on transition matrices showing probability of company migrating from one rating category to another during certain period, based on historical data from rating agencies.
    蒙特卡洛模拟: CreditMetrics涉及执行蒙特卡洛模拟以估计信用损失的概率分布。该分布可用于计算信用VaR。
    Monte Carlo Simulation: CreditMetrics involves carrying out Monte Carlo simulation to estimate probability distribution of credit losses. This distribution can be used to calculate credit VaR.
    🔥 关键优势 - 考虑降级影响:
    Vasicek模型和CreditRisk+估计违约产生的损失概率分布,但未考虑降级的影响。CreditMetrics使用信用评级而非负债定义违约阈值,因此模型考虑了违约和降级的损失。

    Key Advantage - Considers Downgrades:
    Vasicek and CreditRisk+ estimate probability distribution of losses from defaults but impact of downgrades not considered. CreditMetrics defines default threshold using credit ratings not liabilities, thus considers losses from both defaults and downgrades.

    ⚠️ 缺点 | Disadvantage

    • 计算时间密集 | Computationally time-consuming

    Vasicek模型 (Basel II IRB方法) 🌟

    应用: 该模型用于Basel II的内部评级法(IRB)。用于计算贷款组合的VaR。每笔贷款在时间T之前都有一个PD,需要计算在X置信水平下不会超过的最坏情况违约率(WCDR)。

    Application: Used in Basel II's IRB approach. Used for calculating VaR for portfolio of loans. Each loan has a PD by time T, and need to calculate worst case default rate (WCDR) that will not be exceeded at X confidence level.
    最坏情况违约率 | Worst Case Default Rate (WCDR)
    WCDR = N[{N-1(PD) + √ρ × N-1(X)} / √(1-ρ)]

    Where:
    N = cumulative normal distribution
    PD = probability of default
    X = confidence level
    ρ = correlation parameter
    单笔贷款的VaR | VaR for Individual Loan
    VaR = EAD × LGD × WCDR
    Exposure at Default × Loss Given Default × Worst Case Default Rate
    贷款组合的VaR | VaR for Portfolio of Loans
    VaR = Σi EADi × LGDi × WCDRi
    监管资本要求 | Required Regulatory Capital
    监管资本 = VaR - EL
    = Σi EADi × LGDi × (WCDRi - PDi)

    Regulatory Capital = VaR - Expected Loss
    Calculated with T = 1 year and X = 99.9%
    🏦 监管资本参数:
    银行的监管资本使用 T = 1年 和 X = 99.9% 计算。它是预期损失(EL)和VaR之间的差额。

    Regulatory capital for banks is calculated with T = 1 year and X = 99.9%. It is the difference between EL and VaR.

    ⚠️ Vasicek模型的缺陷

    • 纳入的尾部相关性很少 | Incorporates very little tail correlation
    • 可通过替换正态分布来改善 | Replacing normal distribution can remedy this

    CreditRisk+模型

    核心思想: 由瑞士信贷开发的计算CVaR的方法,涉及解析近似。使用类似于保险业计算个别公司违约概率假设的违约损失的程序。

    Core Idea: Methodology to calculate CVaR developed by Credit Suisse involving analytic approximations. Uses similar procedures to insurance industry to calculate default losses from assumptions about probabilities of default of individual companies.

    模型特征 | Model Features

    • 允许对复杂工具组合建模 | Allows modelling of complex portfolios of instruments
    • 违约概率是条件泊松分布 | Default probability is conditionally Poisson distributed
    • 引入违约之间的相关性和依赖性 | Introduces correlation and dependence among defaults
    • 可变违约率和经济趋势周期 | Variable default rates and economic trends/cycles
    违约分布 | Default Distribution:
    • 如果假设违约率已知(无PD波动性): 使用泊松分布确定m次违约的概率
    • 如果假设违约率未知(存在PD波动性): 预期违约次数具有伽马分布,m次违约的概率具有负二项分布

    • If default rate is assumed known (no PD volatility): Use Poisson distribution to determine probability of m defaults
    • If default rate is unknown (PD volatility exists): Expected number of defaults has gamma distribution, probability of m defaults has negative binomial distribution
    相关性效应: 随着违约率的不确定性增加,相关性增加。在违约相关性下,损失概率分布呈正偏。
    Correlation Effect: As uncertainty about default rate increases, correlation increases. With default correlation, loss probability distribution is positively skewed.
    CVaR计算步骤:
    1. 使用泊松、伽马或负二项分布确定组合损失分布
    2. 汇总所有债务人的损失
    3. 使用所需置信区间计算CVaR

    CVaR Calculation Steps:
    1. Determine portfolio loss distribution using Poisson, gamma, or negative binomial distribution
    2. Aggregate losses across all obligors
    3. Calculate CVaR with desired confidence interval

    压力测试 | Stress Testing

    定义: 信用风险压力测试是一种金融风险管理技术,用于评估不利经济条件对金融机构信用投资组合的潜在影响。它涉及模拟极端经济情景并分析这些情景如何影响借款人的信用状况,进而影响机构的整体财务健康。

    Definition: Credit risk stress testing is a financial risk management technique used to assess potential impact of adverse economic conditions on financial institution's credit portfolio. Involves simulating extreme economic scenarios and analyzing how these would affect borrowers' creditworthiness and institution's overall financial health.

    压力测试的关键目标 | Key Goals

    • 识别脆弱性: 指出可能导致重大损失的信用投资组合弱点
      Identify vulnerabilities: Pinpoint weaknesses that could lead to significant losses
    • 评估资本充足性: 确定机构是否持有足够资本以承受极端经济条件
      Assess capital adequacy: Determine if institution holds sufficient capital to withstand extreme conditions
    • 监管合规: 满足压力测试和资本规划的监管要求
      Regulatory compliance: Meet regulatory requirements for stress testing and capital planning
    🌍 气候风险 | Climate Risk:
    气候风险已成为信用风险压力测试的关键因素。包括:
    1. 物理气候风险: 极端天气事件、海平面上升等
    2. 转型风险: 政策变化、市场转变等

    Climate risk has emerged as critical factor in credit risk stress testing, including:
    1. Physical Climate Risks: Extreme weather events, sea level rise, etc.
    2. Transition Risks: Policy changes, market shifts, etc.
    5. 交易对手信用风险 (CCR) Counterparty Credit Risk
    CCR定义: 交易对手信用风险是指在金融交易的现金流最终结算前/合约到期前,交易对手可能违约的风险。如果与交易对手的交易或交易组合在违约时具有净正经济价值,将发生经济损失。CCR创造了双边损失风险:交易的市场价值对任一方都可能是正或负。市场价值是不确定的,可能随着基础市场因素的变动而随时间变化。

    CCR Definition: Counterparty credit risk is the risk that counterparty to a financial transaction could default before final settlement of transaction's cash flows / before contract expiration. Economic loss occurs if transactions or portfolio with counterparty has net positive economic value at time of default. CCR creates bilateral risk of loss: market value of transaction can be positive or negative to either counterparty. Market value is uncertain and can vary over time with movement of underlying market factors.

    降低交易对手风险的方法 | Methods to Reduce Counterparty Risk

    1. 净额结算 | Netting

    定义: 双方之间的协议,对交易进行净额结算:在违约情况下,所有交易被视为单一交易。
    Agreement between two parties to net out transactions: all transactions are considered a single transaction in event of default.

    2. 保证金协议 | Margin Agreements

    定义: 要求双方提供一定金额作为抵押品的协议(折扣);如果未被抵押品覆盖的风险暴露超过阈值。
    Agreement requiring both parties to post certain amount as collateral (haircut); if exposure not covered by collateral exceeds threshold.

    3. 降级条款 | Downgrade Clauses

    定义: 如果交易对手的评级降至某个类别以下,银行有权以市场价值退出合约。
    If counterparty's rating falls below certain category, bank has option to exit contract at market value.

    历史案例: 安然公司 (Enron, 2001)
    Historical Case: Enron, 2001

    4. 中央交易对手 (CCP) | Central Counterparty

    功能: 执行更广泛和更有效的净额结算,要求客户提供保证金。
    Function: Performs broader and more efficient netting, requires margin from clients.

    CVA和DVA 🌟

    信用估值调整 (CVA) | Credit Value Adjustment

    定义: CVA是银行对交易对手违约的预期损失的现值估计。这是由于交易对手违约风险,银行应减少交易价值的金额。

    Definition: CVA is bank's estimate of present value of expected loss to bank of counterparty default. It is amount by which bank should reduce value of transactions because of counterparty default risk.
    CVA计算公式 | CVA Formula
    CVA = Σi qi × vi

    Where:
    qi = default probability at time i
    vi = present value of average net exposure at time i

    债务估值调整 (DVA) | Debit Valuation Adjustment

    定义: DVA是银行自身可能违约的预期收益的现值。这是银行违约给交易对手造成的成本。

    Definition: DVA is present value of expected gain to bank from possibility that it might itself default. It is cost to counterparty of default by bank.
    DVA计算公式 | DVA Formula
    DVA = Σi qi* × vi*

    Where:
    qi* = bank's own default probability at time i
    vi* = present value of average exposure to bank at time i
    组合价值 | Portfolio Value
    组合价值 = 无风险价值 - CVA + DVA
    Portfolio Value = Risk-free Value - CVA + DVA
    ⚠️ DVA的反直觉性质:
    当银行信用状况恶化时,qi*(银行的PD)增加,DVA增加。这使得衍生品组合对银行而言更有价值。当银行信用状况改善时,qi*减少,DVA减少,因此组合价值较低。

    Counterintuitive Nature of DVA:
    As bank's creditworthiness declines, qi* (PD of bank) increases and DVA increases. This makes derivatives portfolio more valuable to bank. As bank's creditworthiness improves, qi* decreases and DVA decreases so portfolio is less valuable.
    💡 重要提示: CVA和DVA必须在银行与交易对手的整个衍生品组合上计算。
    Important Note: CVA and DVA must be calculated on the whole portfolio of derivatives that a bank has with a counterparty.

    错向风险和正向风险 | Wrong-Way Risk and Right-Way Risk

    风险类型 | Risk Type 定义 | Definition 示例 | Example
    错向风险
    Wrong-Way Risk    		 qi与vi呈正相关
    交易对手的违约概率与风险暴露正相关

    qi is positively correlated with vi
    Counterparty's default probability positively correlated with exposure    		 交易对手出售信用保护
    Counterparty is selling credit protection
    正向风险
    Right-Way Risk    		 qi与vi呈负相关
    交易对手的违约概率与风险暴露负相关

    qi is negatively correlated with vi
    Counterparty's default probability negatively correlated with exposure    		 交易对手购买信用保护
    Counterparty is buying credit protection
    🚨 错向风险特别危险! 当交易对手最可能违约时(qi高),银行的风险暴露也最大(vi高),这会显著增加预期损失。

    Wrong-Way Risk is Particularly Dangerous! When counterparty is most likely to default (high qi), bank's exposure is also highest (high vi), significantly increasing expected loss.
    6. 银行监管 (Basel协议) Banking Regulation (Basel Accords)
    监管目的: 银行监管的目的是确保银行为其承担的风险保持足够的资本。银行面临的根本问题是资本充足性的衡量:它们是否有足够的资本来覆盖未来的损失?问题在于系统性风险,即大型银行的失败将导致其他大型银行失败,最终导致金融系统崩溃。

    Purpose of Regulation: Purpose of bank regulation is to ensure banks keep enough capital for risks they take. Fundamental question for banks is measurement of capital adequacy: do they have enough capital to cover future losses? Problem is with systematic risk, i.e., risk that failure by large bank will lead to failure by other large banks and finally financial system.

    巴塞尔协议演变 | Evolution of Basel Accords

    Basel I (1988)

    核心内容: 设定国际风险加权资本充足标准。
    Core Content: Set international risk-based standards for capital adequacy.

    资本充足率 (CAR) | Capital Adequacy Ratio:
    银行资本与风险加权资产(RWA)的比率。银行需要为每个资产类别分配风险权重,反映资产的信用风险。
    资本包括一级资本(普通股、留存收益)和二级资本(次级债务)。

    Ratio of bank's capital to risk-weighted assets (RWA). Banks required to assign risk weight to each asset class, reflecting asset's credit risk.
    Capital consisted of Tier 1 (common stock, retained earnings) and Tier 2 (subordinated debt) capital.
    最低资本要求 | Minimum Capital Requirement
    资本充足率 ≥ 8% of RWA
    其中至少一半来自一级资本

    Capital Adequacy Ratio ≥ 8% of RWA
    At least half from Tier 1 capital

    ⚠️ Basel I批评 | Criticisms

    • 风险敏感性不足 - 监管资本与风险关系不大
    • 权重是临时性的 - 不依赖于银行的风险管理和组合质量
    • 仅处理信用风险
    • "什么是资本"比"应该有多少资本"更重要
  • Not risk-sensitive - regulatory capital has little to do with risk
  • Ad hoc weights - not dependent on bank's risk management and portfolio quality
  • Deals only with credit risk
  • The question "what is capital" is more important than "how much capital"
    •

    Basel II (2004, 2007年实施)

    核心内容: 对信用风险管理和资本充足要求采取更具风险敏感性的方法。
    Core Content: More risk-sensitive approach to credit risk management and capital adequacy requirements.

    三大支柱 | Three Pillars:
    1. 支柱一: 信用风险和操作风险的新最低资本要求
    2. 支柱二: 监管审查过程
    3. 支柱三: 市场纪律

    1. Pillar I: New minimum capital requirements for credit and operational risk
    2. Pillar II: Supervisory review process
    3. Pillar III: Market discipline

    支柱一: 信用风险计量方法 | Pillar I: Credit Risk Measurement Methods

    1. 标准法 (STA) | Standardized Approach

    最简单 外部评级 监管权重
    • 最简单的方法,Basel I的延续
    • 基于外部评级机构对债务人的评级和分配给它的临时风险权重
    • 风险权重由监管机构指定
  • Simplest methodology, continuation of Basel I
  • Based on rating of debtors by external rating agency and ad hoc risk weight assigned
  • Risk weights specified by regulator
    •

    2. 内部评级法 (IRB) | Internal Rating Based Method

    内部模型 监管授权
    核心特征:
    • 基于内部模型/评级
    • 信用VaR公式需经监管机构授权
    • 资本基于1年期VaR,99.9%置信区间
    • 由于银行通常覆盖预期损失(对更差的客户收取更高利率等),所需资本 = VaR - EL

    Core Features:
    • Based on internal models/rating
    • Credit VaR formula subject to supervisory authorization
    • Capital based on 1-year VaR at 99.9% CI
    • Since banks usually cover expected losses (higher rate for worse client), required capital = VaR - EL
    IRB关键参数 | IRB Key Parameters
    • PD (违约概率 | Probability of Default)
    • LGD (违约损失率 | Loss Given Default)
    • EAD (违约风险暴露 | Exposure at Default)
    • M (到期调整因子 | Maturity Adjustment Factor)
    • ρ (相关性) 始终由监管机构设定!

    ρ (correlation) is ALWAYS set by regulators!
    IRB方法 | IRB Approach 银行估计 | Bank Estimates 监管机构设定 | Regulator Sets
    基础IRB
    Foundation IRB    		 PD LGD, EAD, M, ρ
    高级IRB
    Advanced IRB    		 PD, LGD, EAD, M ρ (相关性)

    Basel III (2010)

    背景: 作为对2008年全球金融危机的回应而制定。
    Background: Developed in response to 2008 global financial crisis.

    1. 最低杠杆率 | Minimum Leverage Ratio

    杠杆率要求 | Leverage Ratio Requirement
    一级资本 / 总风险暴露 > 3%

    风险暴露包括:
    • 所有资产负债表内项目
    • 衍生品风险暴露(按Basel I计算)
    • 证券融资风险暴露
    • 某些资产负债表外项目

    Tier 1 Capital / Total Exposure > 3%

    Exposure includes:
    • All on-balance sheet items
    • Derivatives exposures (calculated as in Basel I)
    • Securities financing exposures
    • Some off-balance sheet items

    2. 信用估值调整 (CVA) | Credit Value Adjustment

    应用场景: 用于双边清算,为每个衍生品交易对手计算。
    Application: Used in bilateral clearings, calculated for each derivative counterparty.
    • 定义: 银行因交易对手违约而产生的预期损失
      Expected loss for bank from default of counterparty
    • 会计处理: 报告利润被CVA减少
      Reported profit is reduced by CVA
    • 来源: 来自交易对手信用利差期限结构的平行移动
      Comes from parallel shift in term structure of counterparty's credit spreads
    • 市场风险资本: 信用利差变化产生的CVA需要作为市场风险资本的组成部分
      CVA arising from changing credit spreads required to be component of market risk capital
    错向风险 vs 正向风险:
    • 错向风险: 假设交易对手的PD与对该交易对手的风险暴露之间存在正相关关系;例如交易对手使用CDS向交易商出售保护
    • 正向风险: 假设交易对手的PD与对该交易对手的风险暴露之间存在负相关关系;例如交易对手使用CDS从交易商购买保护

    Wrong-way risk: Positive relationship assumed between PD of counterparty and exposure; e.g., counterparty using CDS to sell protection to dealer
    Right-way risk: Negative relationship assumed between PD of counterparty and exposure; e.g., counterparty using CDS to buy protection from dealer

    3. 债务估值调整 (DVA) | Debit Value Adjustment

    应用场景: 用于双边清算。
    Application: Used in bilateral clearings.
    • 定义: 银行违约给交易对手造成的预期损失
      Expected loss to counterparty from default of bank
    • 本质: 交易对手的CVA
      The counterparty's CVA

    核心公式必须掌握 | Essential Formulas to Master

    1. 预期信用损失 | Expected Credit Loss

    ECL = PD × LGD × EAD

    2. 从CDS利差估计PD | Estimating PD from CDS Spread

    PD(1年) = CDS利差(1年) / LGD
    平均风险率: λ̄(0,t) ≈ s(t) / (1-R)

    3. 默顿模型 | Merton Model

    E0 = V0 × N(d1) - D × e-rT × N(d2)
    PD = N(-d2) = 1 - N(d2)
    DD = d2 (KMV模型的违约距离)

    4. CVA和DVA | CVA and DVA

    CVA = Σ qi × vi
    DVA = Σ qi* × vi*
    组合价值 = 无风险价值 - CVA + DVA

    5. Vasicek模型 | Vasicek Model

    WCDR = N[{N-1(PD) + √ρ × N-1(X)} / √(1-ρ)]
    监管资本 = VaR - EL (T=1年, X=99.9%)
