1. 市场风险基础与希腊字母 Market Risk Fundamentals and Greeks
市场风险定义 | Market Risk Definition:
市场风险是由于不利的价格变动导致金融投资损失的风险。市场风险的例子包括:股票价格变化、大宗商品价格、利率变动或外汇波动。
Market risk is the risk of losses on financial investments caused by adverse price movements. Examples include: changes in equity prices, commodity prices, interest rate moves, or foreign exchange fluctuations.

希腊字母 (Greeks) - 风险敏感性指标

核心作用: 前台通过确保对单个市场变量的敞口不会太高来对冲风险。每个希腊字母衡量交易账簿中风险的不同方面。
Core Function: Front office hedges risks by ensuring exposures to individual market variables are not too high. Each Greek measures a different aspect of risk in a trading book.

Delta (Δ) - 一阶价格敏感性

线性产品 对冲
Delta定义 | Delta Definition
Δ = ∂P/∂S

显示组合对标的资产价格的敏感性
Shows sensitivity of portfolio to price of underlying
线性产品 vs 非线性产品:
• 线性产品 (如现货头寸、远期合约): 价值与标的市场变量线性相关。对冲可以防范大幅和小幅变动("对冲后遗忘")。
• 非线性产品 (如期权): 实现Delta中性只能防范标的资产价格的小幅变动(需要动态对冲、再平衡)。

Linear products (spot position, forward): value linearly dependent on underlying. Hedges protect against large and small changes ("hedge and forget").
Non-linear products (options): achieving delta neutrality only protects against small movements (dynamic hedging, rebalancing needed).
💡 Delta的行为:
• 当股票价格低时,期权的Delta接近0
• 当股票价格高时,行权概率更高,Delta接近1

When stock price is low, option's delta ≈ 0
When stock price is high, probability of exercising is higher, delta ≈ 1

Gamma (Γ) - 二阶价格敏感性

曲率 再平衡频率
Gamma定义 | Gamma Definition
Γ = ∂²P/∂S²

衡量组合Delta相对于标的价格的变化率(需要多频繁再平衡)
Measures rate of change of portfolio's delta with respect to price of underlying (how frequently rebalancing needed)
Gamma的解释:
• 如果Gamma小,Delta变化缓慢
• 如果Gamma大,Delta对标的价格高度敏感
• 期权多头头寸的Gamma为正

• If gamma is small, delta changes slowly
• If gamma is large, delta is highly sensitive to underlying price
• Gamma is positive for long position in option
🔧 实现Gamma中性:
• 线性产品的Gamma为零
• 要使组合Gamma中性,需要非线性产品(如交易期权),头寸为 -Γ/ΓT
• 保持Gamma中性也需要频繁再平衡

• Linear products have zero gamma
• To make portfolio gamma neutral, need non-linear products with position -Γ/ΓT
• Keeping gamma neutrality requires frequent rebalancing

Vega (ν) - 波动率敏感性

隐含波动率 期权定价
Vega定义 | Vega Definition
ν = ∂P/∂σ

组合价值相对于标的资产价格波动率的变化率
Rate of change of portfolio value with respect to volatility of underlying asset price
Vega的特性:
• 如果Vega高,组合对波动率的小幅变化非常敏感
• 期权多头头寸的Vega为正
• 可以通过添加 -ν/νT 的交易期权头寸来改变Vega,从而达到Vega中性

• If vega is high, portfolio is very sensitive to small changes in volatility
• Vega of long position in option is positive
• Can be changed by adding position -ν/νT in traded option to reach vega neutral
⚠️ Gamma和Vega中性的关系:
使用一个期权,Gamma中性的组合不会是Vega中性的,反之亦然。但使用两个期权,可以同时达到两者。
A portfolio that is gamma neutral won't be vega neutral and vice versa with one option, but using two options, we can reach both.
2. 风险价值 (VaR) 与预期损失 (ES) Value at Risk (VaR) and Expected Shortfall (ES)
为什么需要VaR和ES?
金融机构的投资组合通常依赖数百甚至数千个市场变量(如股票指数、利率或大宗商品价格)。因此每天会产生大量风险度量(如Delta、Gamma、Vega)。虽然这些对交易员非常有用,但对高级管理层却不实用。VaR和ES试图用一个数字来总结组合中的总风险。

Why VaR and ES? Financial institution's portfolio often depends on hundreds or thousands of market variables. While Greeks are useful to traders, they're not useful to top management. VaR and ES attempt to provide a single number summarizing total risk in portfolio.

风险价值 (VaR) | Value at Risk

VaR定义 | VaR Definition
VaR = 在正常情况下,在长度为T的时间段内,
以X%置信区间不会超过的损失水平

VaR = Loss level that will not be exceeded during
time period of length T with X% confidence interval
under normal circumstances
VaR提出的问题: "情况会有多糟?"
VaR asks: "How bad can things get?"

VaR的两个关键参数

  • 时间范围 (T): 考察的时间长度 | Time horizon
  • 置信水平 (X%): 概率阈值 | Confidence level
正态分布下的VaR | VaR under Normal Distribution
假设损失~N(μ, σ²),则:
VaR = μ + σ × zα

对于相对短的时间范围,μ通常假设为0:
VaR ∝ σ

Assuming loss~N(μ, σ²):
For relatively short time horizons, μ often assumed to be zero:
VaR is proportional to σ

✓ VaR的优势 | Advantages

  • 用一个数字捕捉风险的重要方面
  • 易于理解和解释
  • 便于回测验证
  • Captures important aspect of risk in single number
  • Easy to understand and interpret
  • Easier to back-test
    •

    ⚠️ VaR的缺陷 | Drawbacks

    • 当VaR用于限制交易员承担的风险时,可能导致不可接受的风险
    • 许多交易策略产生高概率的良好回报和小概率的巨大损失(如大量OTM期权空头)
    • 不满足次可加性,不是一致性风险度量
  • When used to limit trader risks, can lead to unacceptable risk
  • Many strategies give high probability of good returns and small probability of huge loss
  • Does not satisfy subadditivity, not coherent risk measure
    •

    预期损失 (ES) | Expected Shortfall

    ES定义 | ES Definition
    ES = 在时间T内的预期损失/平均损失,
    条件是损失大于损失分布的第X百分位数

    ES = Expected loss / average loss during time T
    conditional on loss being greater than Xth percentile
    of loss distribution
    ES提出的问题: "如果情况真的变糟,预期损失是多少?"
    ES asks: "If things do get bad, what is the expected loss?"
    正态分布下的ES | ES under Normal Distribution
    当损失假设为正态分布N(μ, σ²),置信水平为X的ES为:

    ES = μ + σ × φ(zα) / (1-X)

    其中φ是标准正态PDF,zα是第X百分位数

    当μ=0时: ES ∝ σ

    When loss assumed normally distributed with mean μ and std σ:
    Where φ is standard normal PDF, zα is Xth percentile
    When μ=0: ES is proportional to σ
    💡 为交易员设置ES限制比VaR限制更好:
    这使得他们不太可能承担不可接受的风险。ES考虑了尾部的所有损失,而不仅仅是一个分位数点。
    Setting ES rather than VaR limit for traders makes it less likely they will be able to take unacceptable risks. ES considers all losses in the tail, not just one quantile point.

    ✓ ES的优势 | Advantages

    • 满足所有一致性风险度量的性质
    • 考虑尾部风险,更保守
    • 满足次可加性
    • 更适合监管资本计算
  • Satisfies all coherent risk measure properties
  • Accounts for tail risk, more conservative
  • Satisfies subadditivity
  • Better for regulatory capital calculation
    •

    ⚠️ ES的缺陷 | Drawbacks

    • 不如VaR简单,更难理解
    • 更难回测
    • 计算更复杂
  • Does not have simplicity of VaR, more difficult to understand
  • More difficult to back-test
  • More complex to calculate
    •

    回测 (Back-testing)

    定义: 回测是检验当前计算风险度量程序在过去表现如何的测试。
    Definition: Back-testing is a test of how well the current procedure for calculating risk measure would have worked in the past.

    VaR回测方法 | VaR Back-testing Method

    1. 查看过去可能对我们的投资组合产生巨大负面影响的时期
    2. 检查一天中的损失(对于1天VaR)超过VaR的频率
    3. 这些天被称为"例外"(exceptions)

  • Look at period in past that could have huge negative impact on portfolio
  • Check how often loss in a day exceeded VaR
  • Such days are "exceptions"
    •
    例外的解释:
    • 如果例外只发生几次 → 可以接受
    • 如果例外频繁发生 → 意味着VaR被低估

    Interpreting Exceptions:
    • If exceptions happen only a few times → okay
    • If they happen frequently → VaR is underestimated
    ⚠️ 重要区别: VaR比ES更容易回测。ES作为条件期望值,难以直接验证。
    Important Distinction: VaR is easier to back-test than ES. ES as conditional expectation is difficult to verify directly.
    3. 风险度量的性质 Properties of Risk Measures
    一致性风险度量 (Coherent Risk Measure):
    满足所有四个性质的风险度量称为一致性风险度量。这些性质确保风险度量在数学上合理且符合经济直觉。
    Risk measures satisfying all four properties are called coherent risk measures. These properties ensure the risk measure is mathematically sound and economically intuitive.

    四大性质 | Four Properties

    1. 单调性 (Monotonicity)

    基础性质
    定义: 如果一个组合在每种世界状态下都比另一个组合产生更差的结果,其风险度量应该更大。
    Definition: If a portfolio produces worse result than another portfolio for every state of world, its risk measure should be greater.
    数学表达 | Mathematical Expression
    如果 X ≤ Y (所有状态), 则 ρ(X) ≥ ρ(Y)
    If X ≤ Y (all states), then ρ(X) ≥ ρ(Y)

    2. 平移不变性 (Translation Invariance)

    现金调整
    定义: 如果向组合添加现金金额K,其风险度量应减少K。
    Definition: If amount of cash K is added to portfolio, its risk measure should decrease by K.
    数学表达 | Mathematical Expression
    ρ(X + K) = ρ(X) - K

    添加无风险资产应线性减少风险
    Adding risk-free asset should linearly reduce risk

    3. 正齐次性 (Positive Homogeneity)

    规模效应
    定义: 通过因子λ改变组合规模,保持相对资产权重不变,应导致风险度量乘以λ。
    Definition: Changing portfolio size by factor λ keeping relative asset weights same should lead to risk measure multiplied by λ.
    数学表达 | Mathematical Expression
    ρ(λX) = λρ(X), 对于所有 λ > 0
    ρ(λX) = λρ(X), for all λ > 0

    4. 次可加性 (Subadditivity) 🌟

    分散化收益 最关键
    定义: 两个组合合并后的风险度量不应大于它们单独风险度量的总和。这反映了分散化的好处。
    Definition: Risk measure for two portfolios after merger should be no greater than sum of their risk measures separately. This reflects benefits of diversification.
    数学表达 | Mathematical Expression
    ρ(X + Y) ≤ ρ(X) + ρ(Y)

    组合的风险 ≤ 各部分风险之和
    Risk of combined portfolio ≤ Sum of individual risks
    ⚠️ 这是VaR失败的地方!
    VaR不总是满足次可加性。在某些分布下,VaR可能是超可加的,意味着分散化可能不会减少风险。这是VaR经常受到批评的关键原因。
    This is where VaR fails! VaR does not always satisfy subadditivity. For some distributions, VaR can be super-additive, meaning diversification might not reduce risk. This is a key reason VaR is often criticized.

    常见风险度量的性质评估 | Property Assessment of Common Risk Measures

    风险度量
    Risk Measure    		 单调性
    Monotonicity    		 平移不变性
    Translation Inv.    		 正齐次性
    Homogeneity    		 次可加性
    Subadditivity    		 一致性?
    Coherent?
    VaR ✓ ✓ ✓ ✗ 否 | No
    ES (CVaR) ✓ ✓ ✓ ✓ 是 | Yes
    方差 (Variance) ✗ ✗ ✗ 部分 | Partial 否 | No
    标准差 (Std Dev) ✗ ✗ ✓ 部分 | Partial 否 | No
    🎯 口试要点: 能够解释为什么ES是一致性风险度量而VaR不是。重点是次可加性 - VaR可能惩罚分散化,这在经济上不合理。
    Oral Exam Key Point: Be able to explain why ES is coherent while VaR is not. Focus on subadditivity - VaR may penalize diversification, which is economically unreasonable.

    谱风险度量 (Spectral Risk Measures)

    核心思想: 谱风险度量为损失分布的分位数分配权重。
    Core Idea: Spectral risk measures assign weights to quantiles of loss distribution.

    不同风险度量的权重分配

    • VaR: 对损失分布的第X百分位数分配100%权重
      Assigns 100% weight to Xth percentile of loss distribution
    • ES: 对所有大于第X百分位数的百分位数分配相等权重
      Assigns equal weight to all percentiles greater than Xth percentile
    💡 一致性条件: 如果权重非递减,则谱风险度量是一致的。
    Coherence Condition: Spectral risk measures are coherent if weights are non-decreasing.
    4. VaR和ES的参数选择 Parameter Selection for VaR and ES

    时间范围 (Time Horizon, T)

    选择原则: 适当的时间范围T取决于应用场景。
    Selection Principle: Appropriate choice for time horizon T depends on application.

    不同情况下的时间范围选择

    流动性高、活跃交易的头寸:
    使用短时间范围(可能只有几天)。在这种情况下,μ通常假设为零。
    Very liquid and actively traded positions: Use short time horizon (perhaps only a few days). In such cases, μ is assumed to be zero.
    市场风险考虑:
    分析师通常从1天VaR和ES开始,然后进行缩放。更长的时间范围可能因组合构成的变化而失去意义。
    Market risk consideration: Analysts often start with 1-day VaR and ES, then scale them. Longer horizons may not be meaningful due to changes in portfolio composition.
    时间缩放公式 | Time Scaling Formula
    VaR(T天) = VaR(1天) × √T
    ES(T天) = ES(1天) × √T

    ⚠️ 假设条件:
    • 连续日的组合价值变化具有独立同分布的正态分布
    • 均值为零
    • 在其他情况下,这些是近似值

    These formulas are exactly true when changes in portfolio value on successive days have independent identical normal distributions with mean zero. In other cases, they are approximations.
    📈 时间范围的影响: 随着时间范围增加,VaR和ES都会增加。
    Impact of Time Horizon: As time horizon increases, both VaR and ES increase.

    置信区间 (Confidence Interval, X%)

    选择因素: 置信区间的选择取决于多个因素。
    Selection Factors: Choice of CI depends on number of factors.

    置信区间选择方法

    方法一: 基于信用评级的破产概率
    公司可以根据其信用评级检查特定时期的违约概率(PD),并以1-PD的置信区间计算VaR,以了解需要损失多少才会破产。
    Method 1: Based on Credit Rating Default Probability
    Company could check PD for certain period according to credit rating and calculate VaR at CI of 1-PD to see how much it has to lose to become insolvent.
    方法二: 基于风险态度
    最终这是基于风险态度的选择。
    Method 2: Based on Risk Attitude
    Ultimately it's a choice based on risk attitude.
    ⚠️ 监管标准:
    市场风险的资本要求基于10天99%置信区间的VaR。
    Regulatory Standard: Capital requirement for market risk has been based on 10-day 99% CI VaR.
    📊 置信区间的影响: 随着置信区间增加,VaR和ES都会增加。
    Impact of CI: As CI increases, both VaR and ES increase.

    边际VaR和增量VaR

    边际VaR (Marginal VaR)

    敏感性分析 CAPM联系
    定义: 子组合的边际VaR是VaR对该子组合投资金额的敏感性。
    Definition: Marginal VaR for subportfolio is sensitivity of VaR to amount invested in this subportfolio.
    计算方法:
    通过小幅增加组合中的投资金额,然后重新计算VaR以查看其如何变化。
    Calculation: Increase invested amount in portfolio by small amount, then recalculate VaR to see how it changed.
    💡 与CAPM的关系:
    对于良好分散的投资组合,边际VaR与CAPM的Beta密切相关。
    • 如果资产的Beta高,其边际VaR往往很高
    • 如果Beta低,边际VaR往往很低

    Relationship with CAPM: For well-diversified investment portfolio, marginal VaR is closely related to CAPM's beta.
    • If asset's beta is high, marginal VaR tends to be high
    • If beta is low, marginal VaR tends to be low

    增量VaR (Incremental VaR)

    组合影响
    定义: 增量VaR是包含子组合的VaR与不包含子组合的VaR之间的差异。
    Definition: Incremental VaR is difference between VaR with subportfolio and VaR without subportfolio.
    增量VaR公式
    增量VaR = VaR(组合 + 子组合) - VaR(组合)
    Incremental VaR = VaR(Portfolio + Subportfolio) - VaR(Portfolio)

    VaR和ES的聚合 | Aggregating VaRs and ESs

    VaR聚合公式 | VaR Aggregation Formula
    VaRtotal² = Σi Σj ρij × VaRi × VaRj

    其中:
    • VaRi = 第i部分的VaR
    • VaRtotal = 总VaR
    • ρij = 第i部分和第j部分损失之间的相关性

    Where:
    • VaRi = VaR for ith segment
    • VaRtotal = total VaR
    • ρij = correlation between losses from segment i and j
    适用条件:
    当损失(收益)具有零均值正态分布时,此公式完全正确,在许多其他情况下提供良好近似。当VaR替换为ES时,同样适用。
    Applicability: This is exactly true when losses (gains) have zero-mean normal distributions and provides good approximation in many other situations. Same is true when VaR is replaced by ES.
    5. 不同资产的风险度量方法 Risk Measurement Methods for Different Assets

    股票的VaR | VaR of Stocks

    映射 (Mapping): 可以使用称为映射的程序来计算VaR。这包括将金融工具或组合分解为风险因子。
    Mapping: A procedure called mapping could be used to calculate VaR. This consists of decomposing financial instrument or portfolio into risk factors.

    单一股票的N股组合 | Portfolio with N Identical Shares

    Delta-Normal方法
    VaR计算公式
    VaR = 乘数 × 头寸规模 × σ
    VaR = Multiplier × Position size × σ

    其中 乘数 = N-1(1-CI)
    Where Multiplier = N-1(1-CI)
    假设条件:
    • 股票的风险因子是其收益率
    • 收益率假设为正态分布
    • 如果时间短,μ=0

    Assumptions:
    • Risk factor of share is its return
    • Return assumed to be normally distributed
    • If short horizon, μ=0

    n种不同股票的组合 | Portfolio with n Different Shares

    协方差矩阵
    组合VaR公式
    VaR = 乘数 × σV

    = 乘数 × √(w'Cw)

    = 乘数 × √(wσR)'(wσR)

    Where:
    w = weight vector for each risk factor (share)
    C = covariance matrix
    σR = vector of return volatilities
    关键假设:
    我们计算每个风险因子(股票)在组合中的权重,假设收益率为正态分布,因此组合收益率也是正态分布。
    Key Assumption: Calculate weights for each risk factor (share) in portfolio, returns assumed normally distributed, so portfolio returns are also normally distributed.

    债券的VaR | VaR of Bonds

    核心思想: 债券的风险因子是利率,因此我们需要知道债券价格如何响应利率变动(久期)。
    Core Idea: Risk factors for bonds are interest rates, so we want to know how bond prices change in response to interest rate movements (duration).

    方法一: 久期法 (Duration Approach)

    单一利率 平行移动
    映射到单一利率: YTM或y = r
    Mapping to single interest rate: YTM or y = r
    关键假设:
    • 假设dP和dy之间存在线性关系,假设平行移动
    • 忽略凸性!
    • 假设日收益率正态且独立同分布

    Key Assumptions:
    • Assume linear relationship between dP and dy, assuming parallel shifts
    • Neglects convexity!
    • Assume daily returns are normal and IID
    久期法VaR计算步骤
    1. 计算最坏收益率变动:
    最坏收益率变动 = σ × z统计量

    2. 计算每个债券的久期

    3. 定义潜在利率变化的各种情景

    4. 计算每种情景下每个债券的相应价值变化

    5. 对这些变化求和,估计整个组合价值的总体影响

    6. 对这些情景的潜在组合损失进行排序,
    识别选定置信水平(如95%)的损失,即VaR

    1. Calculate worst yield return = σ × z-statistic
    2. Calculate duration of each bond
    3. Define scenarios for potential interest rate changes
    4. Calculate corresponding change in value for each bond
    5. Sum changes to estimate overall portfolio impact
    6. Rank potential losses and identify loss at chosen CI

    ✓ 优势

    • 简单直观
    • 计算快速

    ⚠️ 局限

    • 忽略凸性
    • 只考虑平行移动
    • 精度较低

    方法二: 现金流映射法 (Cash Flow Mapping)

    收益率曲线 零息债券

    将债券拆分为单独的CF(剥离零息债券)

    3. 通过插值计算所需利率以获得CF的现值

    4. 计算组合的平均收益率和标准差

    5. 获得VaR

    1. Focus on individual cash flows of each bond
    Strip bonds into separate CFs (stripped zero-coupon bonds)
    2. Choose zero bonds with standard maturities
    3. Calculate needed interest rates by interpolation for PV of CFs

    方法三: 主成分分析法 (PCA)

    降维 因子分解
    核心优势: 帮助将由多个到期日组成的收益率曲线的复杂性降低为几个捕捉大部分方差的主要不相关成分。
    Core Advantage: Helps reduce complexity of yield curve consisting of multiple maturities into few main uncorrelated components that capture majority of variance.
    三个主成分 | Three Principal Components:
    1. 第一主成分: 收益率曲线的整体水平(大致平行移动)
    2. 第二主成分: 斜率变化
    3. 第三主成分: 曲率

    1. First PC: Overall level (roughly parallel movement) of yield curve
    2. Second PC: Change in slope
    3. Third PC: Curvature
    PCA方法计算步骤
    1. 以美元表示不同利率1bp变动的组合价值变化

    2. 计算每bp对主成分的敞口

    3. 用于计算因子得分的标准差

    4. 导出VaR计算结果

    1. Express portfolio value change for 1bp movements in different rates in dollars
    2. Calculate exposure to principal components per bp
    3. Use to calculate SD of factor score
    4. Lead to result of VaR calculation

    ✓ 优势

    • 降低维度,简化计算
    • 捕捉收益率曲线的主要变动模式
    • 统计上严格

    ⚠️ 局限

    • 需要历史数据
    • 主成分可能随时间变化
    • 解释性不如直接方法

    6. VaR和ES估计方法 VaR and ES Estimation Methods

    方法一: 历史模拟法 (Historical Simulation) 🌟

    非参数 最流行
    核心思想: 历史模拟是计算市场风险VaR和ES的最流行方法,属于非参数方法。使用过去的数据来预测未来可能发生的情况,这些过去的数据提供分布。
    Core Idea: Being non-parametric method, most popular approach to calculate VaR and ES for market risk. Uses past data to see what could happen in future and these past data provide distribution.

    历史模拟法步骤 | Historical Simulation Steps

    实施步骤 | Implementation Steps
    1. 识别影响组合的市场变量
    (汇率、利率、股票指数等)

    2. 收集最近501天这些市场变量变动的数据
    (提供500个替代情景)

    3. 对于每个情景,计算组合价值从今天到明天的美元变化

    4. 定义每日损失的概率分布
    (收益计为负损失)

    5. 给定每个结果的等概率,
    99百分位数可估计为第5个最坏结果

    6. 计算ES:对损失分布尾部的观察值求平均

    1. Identify market variables affecting portfolio
    2. Collect data on movements in these variables over most recent 501 days
    3. For each scenario, calculate dollar change in portfolio value
    4. Define probability distribution for daily loss
    5. Given equal probability, 99th percentile estimated as 5th worst outcome
    6. Calculate ES: average observations in tail of distribution
    关键假设:
    • 不对分布做任何假设
    • 只假设未来只能像过去那样发生
    • 历史数据决定市场变量的联合概率分布

    Key Assumptions:
    • Do not make any assumptions about distribution
    • Only assume things can only happen in future as they did in past
    • Historical data determine joint probability distribution of market variables

    ✓ 优势 | Advantages

    • 历史数据决定市场变量的联合概率分布(无分布假设)
    • 实施简单直观
    • 自动捕捉尾部风险和非正态性
    • 不需要估计相关性和波动率
  • Historical data determine joint probability distribution (no distribution assumption)
  • Simple and intuitive to implement
  • Automatically captures tail risks and non-normality
  • No need to estimate correlations and volatilities
    •

    ⚠️ 缺陷 | Disadvantages

    • 计算速度比模型构建法慢得多
    • 假设未来会像过去一样
    • 需要大量历史数据
    • 标准误差往往很高
  • Computationally much slower than model-building approach
  • Assumes future will be like past
  • Requires large amount of historical data
  • Standard error tends to be quite high
    •

    历史模拟的扩展 | Extensions of Historical Simulation

    1. 指数加权 | Exponential Weighting
    给予观察值的权重随观察值变老而呈指数递减。
    Weights given to observations decrease exponentially as observations become older.
    2. 波动率调整 | Volatility Adjustment
    对历史数据进行调整以反映波动率的变化。
    Adjustments made to historical data to reflect changes in volatility.
    3. 极值理论 | Extreme Value Theory
    平滑历史模拟计算的组合每日变化概率分布的尾部。导致反映整个尾部形状的VaR和ES估计,而不仅仅是尾部中几个损失的位置。
    Way of smoothing tails of probability distribution of portfolio daily changes. Leads to estimates of VaR and ES that reflect whole shape of tail, not just positions of few losses in tails.
    📊 压力VaR和ES | Stressed VaR and ES:
    可以从历史数据计算压力VaR和ES。迄今为止给出的计算假设在任何给定日期使用最新数据进行历史模拟。然而,历史模拟可以基于过去任何时期的数据。高波动性时期往往会给出高VaR和ES值,而低波动性时期往往会给出低值。

    Stressed VaR and ES can be calculated from historical data. Calculations given so far assumed most recent data used for historical simulation on any given day. However, historical simulations can be based on data from any period in past. Periods of high volatility tend to give high values for VaR and ES, whereas periods of low volatility tend to give low values.
    ⚠️ 标准误差问题:
    使用历史模拟估计的VaR标准误差往往很高。然而,随着样本量增大,误差会减少。所需VaR置信水平越高,标准误差越高。
    Standard Error Issue: Standard error for VaR estimated using historical simulation tends to be quite high. However, as sample gets larger, error will decrease. Higher the VaR confidence level required, higher the standard error.

    方法二: 模型构建法 / 参数法 / 分析法 🌟

    方差-协方差 Delta-Normal
    核心思想: 计算风险度量的替代方法,有时也称为方差-协方差法。这涉及假设市场变量变化的联合分布模型并使用历史数据估计模型参数。我们假设过去的数据是该分布的实现,一个随机样本,因此我们可以使用它们来估计分布。

    Core Idea: Alternative to historical simulation for calculating risk measures, sometimes also referred to as variance-covariance approach. Involves assuming a model for joint distribution of changes in market variables and using historical data to estimate model parameters. We assume past data are realization of this distribution, random sample, so we can use them to estimate distribution.

    Delta-Normal方法

    理想应用场景: 当组合价值的变化与标的市场变量的价值变化呈线性相关时,该模型是理想的。当组合包括期权和其他非线性衍生品时,可以使用蒙特卡洛模拟。
    Ideal Application: Model is ideal when change in portfolio value is linearly dependent on changes in values of underlying market variables. When portfolio includes options and other non-linear derivatives, Monte Carlo simulation can be used.
    损失分布假设 | Loss Distribution Assumption
    如果损失分布是均值为μ,标准差为σ的正态分布,
    VaR和ES可以通过分析确定:

    VaR = μ + σ × zα
    ES = μ + σ × φ(zα)/(1-X)

    If loss distribution is normal with mean μ and std σ,
    VaR and ES can be determined analytically
    线性模型 | Linear Model
    模型假设组合P的价值变化与风险因子(市场变量)的变化线性相关:

    ΔP = Σi δi Δxi

    其中δi是风险因子小幅变化时组合价值的小幅变化

    Model assumes change in portfolio value P is linearly related to changes in risk factors:
    Where δi is small change in portfolio value for small change in risk factor
    计算VaR的要求:
    我们只需要组合变化ΔP的均值和标准差。它使用方差-协方差矩阵来计算组合的风险。在获得波动率后,它使用上述公式来计算风险度量。
    VaR Calculation Requirements: We only need mean and SD of portfolio changes ΔP. It uses variance-covariance matrix to calculate risk of portfolio. After having volatility, it uses formulas above to calculate risk measures.
    💡 与Markowitz组合理论的关系:
    模型构建法基于Harry Markowitz的组合理论。组合价值的均值和标准差可以从标的产品收益率的均值和标准差以及这些收益率之间的相关性计算得出。
    Relationship with Markowitz Portfolio Theory: Model-building approach is based on Harry Markowitz's portfolio theory. Mean and standard deviation of portfolio value can be calculated from mean and standard deviation of returns on underlying products and correlations between those returns.

    ✓ 优势 | Advantages

    • 计算速度比历史模拟快得多
    • 基于成熟的组合理论
    • 便于解释和实施
    • 适合线性产品
  • Computationally much faster than historical simulation
  • Based on established portfolio theory
  • Easy to interpret and implement
  • Suitable for linear products
    •

    ⚠️ 缺陷 | Disadvantages

    • 假设风险因子的每日变化具有多元正态分布,而实际情况往往并非如此
    • 对非线性产品(如期权)不太准确
    • 可能低估尾部风险
  • Daily changes in risk factors assumed to have multivariate normal distribution which is often not the case
  • Less accurate for non-linear products (options)
  • May underestimate tail risks
    •
    典型应用:
    模型构建法最常用于投资组合(毕竟它与流行的Markowitz均值-方差组合分析方法密切相关)。它不太常用于计算金融机构交易操作的VaR。这是因为金融机构喜欢保持其相对于市场变量的Delta接近零。
    Typical Application: Model-building approach most often used for investment portfolios. (After all, closely related to popular Markowitz mean-variance method of portfolio analysis.) Less commonly used for calculating VaR for trading operations of financial institution. This is because financial institutions like to maintain their deltas with respect to market variables close to zero.

    方法三: 蒙特卡洛模拟 (Monte Carlo Simulation)

    随机模拟 非线性产品
    定义: 一种数值方法,通过从已知分布生成许多随机数来产生概率分布。
    Definition: Numerical method that produces probability distribution by generating many random numbers from known distribution.
    💡 应用场景: 特别适合包含期权和其他非线性衍生品的组合,以及模型构建法假设不成立的情况。
    Application Scenario: Particularly suitable for portfolios containing options and other non-linear derivatives, and when model-building approach assumptions don't hold.
    7. 资本要求与FRTB Capital Requirements and Fundamental Review of the Trading Book

    1996年修正案 | 1996 Amendment

    重大变革: 引入了市场风险资本要求。
    Major Change: Introduced capital for market risk.

    两种方法 | Two Approaches

    1. 标准化方法 (适用于简单银行)
    Standardized Approach (Simpler Banks)

    监管机构规定如何计算市场风险;为每种类型的市场敞口(债务证券、股票证券、外汇风险、商品风险和期权)单独分配资本。
    Regulators specified how to compute market risk; capital assigned separately to each type of market exposure (debt securities, equity securities, FX risk, commodity risk and options).
    2. 内部模型法 (适用于复杂银行)
    Internal Model Approach (More Sophisticated Banks)

    计算VaR(10天99%置信水平)并将其转换为资本要求。
    Calculating VaR (10-day 99% confidence level) and converting it to capital requirement.
    ⚠️ 要求VaR回测:
    计算过去250天的1天99% VaR并计算例外次数,以查看VaR是否被低估。
    Required VaR Back-testing: Calculating 1-day 99% VaR over previous 250 days and counting exceptions to see whether VaR is underestimated.
    总资本要求 | Total Capital Requirement
    总资本要求 = 信用风险8%资本 + 市场风险资本
    Total Capital Required = 8% capital from credit risk + capital from market risk

    交易账簿基本审查 (FRTB) 🌟

    定义: 这是一份提出对计算市场风险监管资本方式进行重大修订的文件,以更复杂的方式确定资本。
    Definition: Document proposing major revisions to way regulatory capital for market risk is calculated, determining capital in much more complex way.

    从Basel I到FRTB的演变 | Evolution from Basel I to FRTB

    Basel I问题:
    市场风险资本计算基于10天期限,99%置信水平的VaR。问题是它们通常基于之前的时期(1-4年)且未进行回测。
    Basel I Issues: Market risk capital calculations based on VaR calculated for 10-day horizon with 99% confidence level. Problem was they were typically based on previous period (1-4 years) and were not back-tested.
    Basel II.5改进:
    除当前度量外,还要求银行计算"压力VaR"度量,这意味着搜索250天期间,观察到的市场变量变动将导致当前组合面临重大财务压力。
    Basel II.5 Improvement: Required banks to calculate "stressed VaR" measure in addition to current measure, which meant searching for 250-day period where observed movements in market variables would lead to significant financial stress for current portfolio.
    🔥 FRTB的核心变革:
    随Basel III引入,提议使用97.5%压力ES。
    • 在正态分布中,99% VaR和97.5% ES几乎相同
    • 在非正态分布(如重尾)中,ES更大
    • ES的时间范围基于风险因子(市场变量:利率、信用利差、股票价格等)的流动性类型
    • ES没有回测,因为它已经是压力度量

    FRTB Core Changes: Introduced with Basel III, proposed use of 97.5% stressed ES.
    • In normal distribution, 99% VaR and 97.5% ES are almost same
    • In non-normal distributions like heavy tails, ES is greater
    • Time horizon used for ES is based on type of risk factor's liquidity
    • No back-testing for ES because it's already stressed measure

    FRTB的两种方法 | Two FRTB Approaches

    1. 标准化方法 | Standardized Approach
    资本要求 = 风险费用(使用风险敏感性方法计算) + 违约风险费用 + 剩余风险附加费
    Capital requirement = Risk charge (calculated using risk sensitivity approach) + Default risk charge + Residual risk add-on
    2. 内部模型法 | Internal Models Approach
    • 使用ES代替VaR
    • 风险因子被分配到时间范围
    • 然后计算流动性调整后的ES

    • ES instead of VaR
    • Risk factors allocated to time horizons
    • Then liquidity adjusted ES calculated
    ⚠️ 交易台层面计算:
    计算在交易台层面进行,因此可能发生的情况是,例如,银行的外汇交易台使用标准化方法,而股票交易台被允许使用内部方法。
    Trading Desk Level Calculations: Calculations carried out at trading desk levels, so it may happen that, for example, bank's foreign currency trading desk uses standardized approach while equity trading desk is allowed to use internal approach.
