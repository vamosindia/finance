1. 完美资本市场假设 Perfect Capital Markets — Underlying Assumptions

完美资本市场是 MM 定理的理论前提，包含三个核心条件。
Perfect capital markets form the foundational assumption set of the MM theorem.
  1. 同价交易 / Same Set of Securities at Competitive Prices：
    投资者和企业能够以等于未来现金流现值的竞争性市场价格交易同一组证券。
    Investors and firms can trade the same set of securities at competitive market prices equal to the present value of their future cash flows.
  2. 无摩擦成本 / No Frictions：
    不存在税收、交易成本或证券发行成本。
    There are no taxes, transaction costs, or issuance costs associated with security trading.
  3. 融资中性 / Financing-Neutrality：
    企业的融资决策不会改变投资产生的现金流，也不会泄露关于这些现金流的新信息（不存在信息不对称）。
    A firm's financing decisions do not change the cash flows generated by its investments, nor do they reveal new information about them.

2. 资本结构基础 Capital Structure Fundamentals

资本结构（Capital Structure）：企业已发行在外的债务、股权及其他证券的相对比例。
Capital structure is the relative proportions of debt, equity, and other securities a firm has outstanding.

企业可以选择：

  • 全股权融资 / All-equity financing — 此时股权称为无杠杆股权 (unlevered equity)
  • 部分债务融资 / Partial debt financing — 此时股权称为有杠杆股权 (levered equity)

3. 无杠杆 vs 有杠杆股权（数值示例） Unlevered vs Levered Equity — A Numerical Walkthrough

假设一家企业承担一个有市场风险的项目，无风险利率 rf = 5%，风险溢价 = 10%，故 rU = 15%。

A firm undertakes a risky project with rf = 5%, risk premium = 10%, so rU = 15%.

项目现金流 / Project CF1,4009001,150
无杠杆股权 / Unlevered Equity CF1,4009001,150
无杠杆股权价值 / Value of Unlevered Equity VU = E[CF] / (1 + rU) = 1,150 / 1.15 = $1,000

若企业借入 $500（无风险，rD = 5%），则需支付 $525 给债权人：

情景经济好经济差期望值
债务现金流 / Debt CF525525525
有杠杆股权现金流 / Levered Equity CF875375625
由"一价定律"，债权与股权之和等于项目（资产）总价值：VL = D + E = $1,000 = VU。债务部分价值 = 525/1.05 = $500，因此有杠杆股权价值 = 1,000 - 500 = $500。
By the Law of One Price, VL = D + E = $1,000 = VU. With D = 500, levered equity is worth $500.
一价定律 (Law of One Price)：在竞争市场中同时交易的相同投资机会必须有相同价格——因为投资者会迅速套利。
Identical investment opportunities trading simultaneously in competitive markets must have the same price, because investors arbitrage rapidly.
由于债权人优先受偿，有杠杆股权风险更高，投资者要求更高的预期回报。"债务便宜"会被股权成本上升所抵消，最终公司总资本成本不变。
Because debtholders are paid first, levered equity is riskier and demands higher returns. The "cheap debt" effect is offset by rising cost of equity, leaving total cost of capital unchanged.

4. MM 定理 I：价值不变定理 MM Proposition I — The Irrelevance Result

MM I：在完美资本市场中，企业的总市场价值由其资产产生的现金流决定，与其资本结构无关。
In perfect capital markets, the total market value of a firm equals the market value of the cash flows generated by its assets and is independent of its capital structure.
MM I 公式 / MM I Formula VL = VU = E + D

逻辑推理 / Reasoning

  1. 无税无成本时，资产产生的全部现金流可分配给所有证券持有人。
  2. 因此证券组合的总价值 = 资产总价值（一价定律）。
  3. 无论债务/股权比例如何切割"现金流的派"，派的总大小不变。
核心隐喻：资本结构如同把披萨切成几块——不论怎么切，披萨的总量不变。
Metaphor: Capital structure is like slicing a pizza — no matter how you slice it, the total size remains the same.

5. 自制杠杆 / Homemade Leverage

如果投资者偏好与企业不同的资本结构，他们可以在自己的投资组合中通过借贷来达到所需杠杆水平。
If investors want a different capital structure than the firm's, they can borrow or lend on their own to achieve their preferred leverage in their portfolios.
投资者偏好 / Preference企业资本结构投资者操作
想要更多杠杆全股权融资融资买入（保证金）/ Buy on margin
想要更少杠杆有杠杆买入企业的债权+股权 / Buy both D & E
由于投资者可以自行复制任何资本结构，企业的融资选择不会影响投资者的机会集，因此资本结构不创造价值。
Since investors can replicate any capital structure on their own, the firm's choice does not change the opportunity set, so capital structure does not create value.

6. 市场价值资产负债表与杠杆性资本重组 Market Value Balance Sheet & Leveraged Recapitalization

市场价值资产负债表 / Market Value Balance Sheet

  • 包含所有资产和负债，包括无形资产（声誉、品牌、人力资本）；
  • 使用当前市场价值而非历史成本；
  • 体现"价值由资产投资选择创造"的理念——选择正 NPV 项目方能增加企业价值。

杠杆性资本重组 / Leveraged Recapitalization

企业用新发行债务的所得回购股票。在完美市场中，每股价格不变，因为资产和负债等量增加。
A firm buys back shares with proceeds from newly issued debt. Share price should not change since both assets and liabilities increase by the same amount.

7. MM 定理 II：股权成本与杠杆 MM Proposition II — Cost of Levered Equity

MM II：有杠杆企业的股权资本成本随债务-股权比 (D/E) 线性递增。
The cost of capital of levered equity rises linearly with the firm's market-value debt-equity ratio (D/E).
MM II 核心公式 / Core Formula rE = rU + (D/E)(rU − rD)

各项含义 / Notation

符号含义 / Meaning
rU = rA无杠杆股权回报率 = 资产回报率 / Unlevered cost of equity = cost of assets
rE有杠杆股权成本 / Cost of levered equity
rD债务成本 / Cost of debt
D/E市场价值债务股权比 / Market-value debt-equity ratio

推导思路 / Derivation

由资产 = 债权+股权 的组合公式：

rU = (E/(E+D))·rE + (D/(E+D))·rD

对 rE 求解即得 MM II。

额外溢价 (rU − rD) × D/E 补偿股东承担的额外财务风险。即使债务无违约风险，杠杆也会增加股权风险。
The extra premium (rU − rD) × D/E compensates shareholders for additional financial risk. Even riskless debt raises equity risk.

8. WACC 与资本结构 / WACC & Capital Structure

完美市场（无税）下的 WACC / Pre-tax WACC WACC = (E/(E+D))·rE + (D/(E+D))·rD = rU = rA
在完美资本市场中，WACC 与资本结构无关，等于无杠杆资本成本（资产回报率）。
In perfect markets, WACC is independent of capital structure and equals the unlevered cost of capital (the cost of assets).

直觉理解 / Intuition

  • 债务比重↑ → 债务和股权都变更risky → 各自资本成本↑；
  • 但更多权重压在较便宜的债务上 → 加权平均刚好抵消；
  • 结果：WACC 是水平直线（在 MM 完美世界中）。
现实世界中 WACC 通常呈轻微 U 形——存在一个最优资本结构使 WACC 最小。这正是 MM 定理的"反推"价值：偏离 MM 必有摩擦。
In reality, WACC is slightly U-shaped — there's an optimal capital structure that minimizes WACC. Any deviation from MM signals a market friction.

9. 资产 Beta（无杠杆 Beta） Unlevered (Asset) Beta

资产 Beta = 加权平均 βU = (E/(E+D))·βE + (D/(E+D))·βD
资产 Beta 衡量企业基础资产的市场风险，可用于评估可比投资的资本成本。
Asset beta measures the market risk of the firm's underlying assets and can be used to assess the cost of capital for comparable investments.

净债务概念 / Net Debt

现金和无风险证券降低资产风险，被视为"负债务"。评估企业价值时使用净债务 = 总债务 − 多余现金/短期投资。
Cash and risk-free securities reduce asset risk and act as "negative debt." Use net debt = total debt − excess cash/short-term investments.

10. 资本结构常见谬误 / Capital Structure Fallacies

谬误 1：杠杆能提高 EPS / Leverage increases EPS

事实：杠杆确实可在收益高时提高 EPS，但在收益低时也降低 EPS。EPS 的风险同时上升，股东要求更高回报，正好抵消，每股价格不变。
Truth: Leverage raises EPS in good states but lowers it in bad states. The risk of EPS rises proportionally, and shareholders demand higher returns. Effects cancel — share price is unchanged.

谬误 2：发行新股稀释股东 / Equity issuance dilutes shareholders

事实：只要新股以公允价格发行，募集的现金会等量增加资产，不存在价值损失。
Truth: As long as new shares are sold at a fair price, cash raised increases assets equally — no gain or loss to existing shareholders.

11. 价值守恒原理 / Conservation of Value Principle

在完美资本市场中，金融交易既不创造也不消灭价值，仅是风险（与对应回报）的重新打包。
In perfect capital markets, financial transactions neither create nor destroy value; they merely repackage risk (and corresponding return).

因此 MM 定理的"反向"价值在于：在现实世界中观察到资本结构对企业价值有影响，那就必然是因为存在某种市场摩擦（税收、破产成本、信息不对称、代理问题等）。

12. 引入税收：债务税盾 Debt and Taxes — Interest Tax Shield

利息支出可抵扣应税收入，给债务带来税收优势。
Interest expenses are tax-deductible, giving debt a tax advantage.
利息税盾 / Interest Tax Shield ITS = τc × 利息支出 / τc × Interest Expense

含税 MM I：调整后定理 / MM I With Taxes

VL = VU + PV(ITS)

若债务永续且税率不变：

PV(ITS) = τc × D

有效税后借款利率 / Effective After-Tax Cost of Debt

rDafter-tax = rD(1 − τc)

含税 WACC / After-Tax WACC

WACC = (E/(E+D))·rE + (D/(E+D))·rD·(1 − τc)
与 MM II 不同，引入税收后，WACC 随债务比例上升而下降（因税盾增加）。
Unlike MM II, with taxes the after-tax WACC declines as debt increases (tax shield grows).

13. 个人税与有效债务税收优势 Personal Taxes & Effective Tax Advantage

投资者收到现金流后还要再次纳税。个人税可能部分抵消企业层面的债务税收优势。
Investors are taxed again upon receiving cash flows. Personal taxes can partially offset corporate-level debt tax advantages.
有效债务税收优势 / Effective Tax Advantage τ* = 1 − [(1 − τc)(1 − τe)] / (1 − τi)
符号 / Symbol含义 / Meaning
τc企业所得税率 / Corporate tax rate
τe股权收益个人税率（股息+资本利得）/ Personal tax on equity income
τi利息收入个人税率 / Personal tax on interest income

14. 最优资本结构（含税视角） Optimal Capital Structure with Taxes

  1. 从纯税收角度，最优杠杆水平是利息支出 = EBIT（再多则税盾失效）；
  2. 由于 EBIT 不确定，企业应让利息低于预期 EBIT以保持安全边际；
  3. 盈利增长率越高，股权价值越高，最优 D/(D+E) 比例越低；
  4. 实际企业不会到达"税收最优"，因为债务还有破产成本、代理成本等其他代价。
这导出"权衡理论 (Trade-off Theory)"：最优资本结构在债务税盾收益与财务困境成本之间取得平衡。
This leads to the Trade-off Theory: optimal capital structure balances tax shield benefits against financial distress costs.

15. 口试可能问题精选 / Likely Oral Exam Questions

Q1. 基础 请陈述 MM 定理 I 并解释其前提假设。
State MM Proposition I and its underlying assumptions.
MM 定理 I 指出：在完美资本市场中，企业总市场价值由资产产生的现金流决定，独立于其资本结构，即 VL = VU。三大前提：(1) 投资者与企业按竞争性市场价格交易同套证券；(2) 不存在税收、交易成本或发行成本；(3) 融资决策不改变现金流，也不传递新信息。
MM I states that in perfect capital markets, the total market value of a firm equals the market value of its asset cash flows and is independent of capital structure (VL = VU). Assumptions: (1) investors and firms trade the same securities at competitive prices, (2) no taxes, transaction or issuance costs, (3) financing is cash-flow and information neutral.
Q2. 基础 为何完美市场中资本结构对企业价值无影响？请用直觉/类比解释。
Why is capital structure irrelevant in perfect markets? Give intuition.
"披萨类比"：企业资产产生的现金流就像一个披萨，债权与股权只是切披萨的不同方式，无论怎么切，披萨总量不变。同时由"一价定律"，组合（D+E）必须等于其复制对象（资产）的价值。
The "pizza analogy": cash flows are the pizza; debt and equity are simply different ways of slicing it. Total size is unchanged. The Law of One Price ensures D + E equals the asset value they replicate.
Q3. 基础 推导 MM 定理 II 公式。
Derive the MM Proposition II formula.
资产组合等价：rU = (E/V)·rE + (D/V)·rD，其中 V = E + D。
解出 rE：rE = rU + (D/E)(rU − rD)。
含义：股权成本随 D/E 线性增加，斜率等于 (rU − rD)。
Portfolio equivalence: rU = (E/V)·rE + (D/V)·rD. Solving for rE: rE = rU + (D/E)(rU − rD). Cost of equity rises linearly in D/E with slope (rU − rD).
Q4. 进阶 什么是自制杠杆？它在 MM 定理证明中扮演什么角色？
What is homemade leverage and its role in MM's proof?
自制杠杆指投资者通过个人借贷在自己的投资组合中复制任何想要的杠杆水平。它是 MM I 套利论证的关键：如果 VL ≠ VU，投资者可借助自制杠杆构造无风险套利组合。因此企业资本结构的选择不影响投资者的机会集，进而不创造价值。
Homemade leverage refers to investors replicating any desired leverage in their portfolios via personal borrowing/lending. It's central to the MM I no-arbitrage proof: if VL ≠ VU, investors can construct riskless arbitrage. Hence capital structure does not expand investors' opportunity set or create value.
Q5. 基础 为何"债务比股权便宜"是误导性说法？
Why is "debt is cheaper than equity" misleading?
单看 rD < rE 确实如此，但债务增加使股权风险提高 → rE 上升。两者按权重平均后，WACC 在完美市场中保持不变。"便宜"的债务的代价是"更贵"的股权。
Although rD < rE, adding debt raises equity risk and rE. The weighted average WACC remains unchanged in perfect markets. The "cheap debt" comes at the cost of "more expensive equity."
Q6. 进阶 杠杆性资本重组中股价为何不变？
Why doesn't share price change in a leveraged recap?
企业用新借的债务回购股票：资产端现金先增加（来自借款）然后减少（用于回购），负债端债务增加。市场价值资产负债表两边等量变化，剩余股东每股权益价值不变。
The firm raises debt and uses proceeds to repurchase shares. Assets first rise (cash from debt) then fall (paid for buyback); liabilities rise by the same debt amount. Both sides of the market-value balance sheet change equally — per-share value of remaining equity is unchanged.
Q7. 易混 MM 定理在含税情况下如何修改？
How does MM change when corporate taxes are introduced?
引入企业所得税后，利息支出可抵税，产生利息税盾 (ITS) = τc × 利息。
修正后的 MM I：VL = VU + PV(ITS)。永续债假设下 PV(ITS) = τc × D。
修正后的 WACC：WACC = (E/V)rE + (D/V)rD(1−τc)，并且 WACC 随杠杆增加而下降。
With corporate taxes, interest is deductible, creating an Interest Tax Shield (ITS = τc × interest). Modified MM I: VL = VU + PV(ITS). For perpetual debt, PV(ITS) = τcD. After-tax WACC decreases with leverage.
Q8. 进阶 个人税如何影响债务的税收优势？
How do personal taxes affect debt's tax advantage?
投资者从股权（股息+资本利得）和债权（利息）获得的收入面临不同个人税率。利息通常按普通收入税率（较高），股权收入往往享受更低税率，部分抵消企业税层面的债务优势。
有效税收优势：τ* = 1 − [(1−τc)(1−τe)] / (1−τi)。
Investors face different personal tax rates on equity (dividends + capital gains) and debt (interest). Interest is usually taxed at higher ordinary rates while equity income enjoys lower rates, partially offsetting corporate-level debt advantages. Effective τ* = 1 − [(1−τc)(1−τe)] / (1−τi).
Q9. 进阶 从纯税收角度，最优杠杆水平是多少？为何现实中企业不会达到此水平？
What is the tax-optimal leverage and why don't firms reach it in practice?
纯税收最优：使利息支出 ≈ EBIT（更多债务无新增税盾，因抵扣超过应税收入）。
现实中无法达到：
(1) EBIT 不确定，需安全边际；
(2) 高债务带来财务困境成本和破产成本；
(3) 代理问题（资产替代、投资不足等）；
(4) 信息不对称影响发行成本。
这就是权衡理论的核心。
Tax-optimal leverage: interest ≈ EBIT (further debt yields no new shield). In practice firms stop short due to (1) EBIT uncertainty requiring a buffer, (2) financial distress and bankruptcy costs, (3) agency costs (asset substitution, underinvestment), (4) asymmetric information. This is the trade-off theory.
Q10. 基础 解释"价值守恒原理"。
Explain the Conservation of Value Principle.
在完美资本市场中，金融交易既不创造也不消灭价值，仅重新打包风险与回报。企业改变债权-股权组合时，各证券的风险和预期回报会变化，但总资本成本不变。这一原理的"反推"价值在于：现实中观察到资本结构影响价值，必由市场摩擦（税、破产成本、信息不对称、代理成本等）造成。
In perfect markets, financial transactions neither add nor destroy value — they merely repackage risk and return. When debt/equity mix changes, individual securities' risk and returns adjust but overall cost of capital is unchanged. The principle's converse value: any observed effect of capital structure on firm value must arise from market frictions (taxes, bankruptcy costs, asymmetric information, agency costs).
Q11. 易混 EPS 提升 = 股价上升？
Does higher EPS imply a higher share price?
不一定。当借入成本低于预期收益/股价比时，杠杆会提高预期 EPS。但杠杆同时放大 EPS 的波动性，股东要求更高回报。两效应在完美市场恰好相互抵消，股价不变。
Not necessarily. Leverage raises expected EPS when after-tax borrowing cost is below the earnings/price ratio, but it also amplifies EPS volatility, raising required equity returns. In perfect markets these effects cancel — share price unchanged.
Q12. 进阶 资产 Beta 的含义是什么？为何要使用净债务？
What is asset beta? Why use net debt?
资产 Beta（无杠杆 Beta）衡量企业基础资产的市场风险，等于股权和债权 Beta 的市值加权平均。它独立于资本结构，可用于评估可比项目的资本成本（如同业比较）。
使用净债务因为：现金和短期投资降低公司整体风险，相当于"负债务"，应予扣除以反映真实经营杠杆。
Asset beta (unlevered beta) measures the market risk of a firm's underlying assets — the market-value-weighted average of equity and debt betas. It's independent of capital structure and is useful for cost-of-capital estimation in comparables. Net debt is used because cash and risk-free securities lower overall risk and act as "negative debt," so they're netted out to reflect operating leverage.
Q13. 进阶 MM 定理的"反向"价值在于什么？
What is the "reverse" value of the MM theorem?
MM 定理本身基于完美市场假设，结论看似不切实际。但其真正贡献在于提供了一个基准：现实中观察到的资本结构对企业价值的影响，必然源于某些市场摩擦。后续理论（权衡理论、优序融资理论、市场择时理论等）正是通过逐一识别这些摩擦来扩展 MM 框架。
Though MM rests on idealized assumptions, its true contribution is providing a benchmark: any real-world effect of capital structure on firm value must come from market frictions. Subsequent theories — trade-off, pecking order, market timing — extend MM by identifying these frictions one by one.
Q14. 易混 什么情况下含税 WACC 公式不再适用？
When does the after-tax WACC formula break down?
含税 WACC 公式 WACC = (E/V)rE + (D/V)rD(1−τc) 假设：每期税盾恰等于 D × rD。但如果存在非全额可抵扣的债务利息（如部分次级债），或 EBIT 不足以全额吸收税盾，公式即失效。同时该公式假设股权融资无税盾效应。
The after-tax WACC formula assumes period tax shield equals D × rD. It breaks down when interest is not fully deductible (e.g., subordinated debt) or when EBIT is insufficient to absorb the full shield. It also assumes equity financing carries no tax shield.
Q15. 进阶 增长率如何影响最优杠杆？
How does earnings growth rate affect optimal leverage?
增长率越高 → 股权价值（PV 未来现金流）越高 → 在固定 D 下 D/(D+E) 越低。因此高增长企业的最优杠杆比率较低。直觉：高增长企业更多价值来自未来增长机会而非当前现金流，而税盾的价值依赖当前应税收入，所以高增长企业相对受益较少。
Higher growth → higher equity value (PV of future CFs) → lower D/(D+E) at given D. So high-growth firms have lower optimal leverage. Intuition: their value comes more from future opportunities than current cash flows, while tax shield benefits depend on current taxable income.
Q16. 易混 完美市场中 WACC 是水平直线，现实中却呈 U 形，为什么？
Why is WACC flat in perfect markets but U-shaped in reality?
完美市场中：股权和债务成本上升被加权平均机制抵消，WACC 水平。
现实中：(1) 税盾使 WACC 起初下降；(2) 但杠杆过高时财务困境成本、代理成本、违约风险溢价急剧上升，使 rD 和 rE 不再是 MM 线性关系，WACC 开始上升。两者结合形成 U 形，最低点即最优资本结构。
In perfect markets, rising rE exactly offsets the lower-cost debt weight, keeping WACC flat. In practice, (1) tax shields lower WACC initially, but (2) at high leverage, financial distress costs, agency costs, and default premiums spike, making rD and rE grow non-linearly. Combined, this yields a U-shape with the minimum at the optimal capital structure.
Q17. 进阶 什么是市场价值资产负债表？为什么用它而不是会计资产负债表？
What is the market value balance sheet, and why use it?
市场价值资产负债表(1) 包括无形资产（品牌、声誉、人力资本）—会计资产负债表通常不予列报；(2) 使用当前市场价值而非历史成本。在评估资本结构决策时，必须用市场价值，因为资本市场依据市值定价证券，且 MM 推论以市值为基础。
The market value balance sheet (1) includes intangibles (brand, reputation, human capital) often missing from accounting reports, and (2) uses current market values, not historical costs. It's essential for capital structure analysis because securities are priced at market values and MM results are stated in market terms.
Q18. 易混 为什么 rU 也称为 rA？
Why is rU also called rA?
无杠杆股权完全代表企业资产，因为该企业所有资产现金流都流向股东。所以无杠杆股权回报率 = 资产回报率。在 MM 完美市场中，rU = rA = WACC，不依赖资本结构。
Unlevered equity claims all asset cash flows, so the unlevered cost of equity equals the cost of assets. In MM's perfect markets, rU = rA = WACC, independent of capital structure.
Q19. 进阶 哪些因素使现实公司偏离 MM 的"无关性"？
What real-world frictions cause deviations from MM irrelevance?
主要包括：
(1) 税收：利息税盾（企业税）vs 个人税；
(2) 财务困境与破产成本：直接（律师、法庭费用）和间接（客户、供应商、员工流失）；
(3) 代理问题：资产替代（股东转向高风险投资）、投资不足（拒绝有正 NPV 但收益归债权人的项目）、自由现金流问题；
(4) 信息不对称：导致优序融资（内部资金 → 债务 → 股权）；
(5) 市场择时：企业利用过高股价发行股权；
(6) 发行成本与交易成本。
Key frictions: (1) Taxes — interest tax shield vs personal taxes; (2) Financial distress and bankruptcy costs — direct (legal/court fees) and indirect (loss of customers, suppliers, staff); (3) Agency conflicts — asset substitution, underinvestment, free cash flow problems; (4) Asymmetric information — leading to pecking order (internal → debt → equity); (5) Market timing — issuing equity when overpriced; (6) Issuance and transaction costs.
Q20. 进阶 MM 定理与现代资本结构理论之间的关系？
Relationship between MM theorem and modern capital structure theories?
MM 是现代资本结构理论的起点和基准。现代理论通过逐一引入 MM 假设的违反，构建更现实的模型：
• 权衡理论 (Trade-off Theory)：税盾收益 vs 财务困境成本，给出最优资本结构；
• 优序融资理论 (Pecking Order, Myers-Majluf)：信息不对称下，内部资金 > 债务 > 股权；
• 市场择时理论 (Market Timing)：企业根据市场估值高低择机融资；
• 代理理论 (Agency Theory, Jensen-Meckling)：债务作为约束管理层的工具。
所有这些理论本质上都是对 MM 假设的"放松"。
MM is the starting point and benchmark of modern capital structure theory. Modern theories build on relaxing MM assumptions: Trade-off Theory (tax shield vs distress costs), Pecking Order (Myers-Majluf, asymmetric info: internal > debt > equity), Market Timing (issuing when valuations favor), and Agency Theory (Jensen-Meckling, debt disciplines management). All extend MM by introducing specific frictions.
